**What Starting Monday gives coaches - one page**

## Core features

### Pre-Session Snapshot
Before each client session, view a single-screen summary of what has changed since your last conversation: new signals on tracked companies, pipeline movements, confidence score change, commitments kept or missed, and one recommended talking point for today's session. Reduces context-rebuild time from 20-30 minutes to under 10 minutes.

### Client Portfolio Dashboard
A portfolio-level view of all your clients: active vs. drifting, confidence trend, pipeline velocity, prep quality score, and last session date. Surface the clients who need intervention before problems compound. Prioritize your limited attention on the people who are quietly losing momentum.

### Client Visibility Controls (client-controlled)
Your clients set what you can see. Options include: read-only signal view, pipeline access, full prep brief access, or no access. You cannot see anything a client has not granted. This design keeps your coaching authority intact and eliminates the risk of the platform being perceived as surveillance.

### Session Commitment Tracker
Log commitments made in each session (actions the client agreed to take before your next call). The platform tracks follow-through and surfaces commitments that are at risk or overdue. Walk into the next session knowing exactly whether the plan was executed.

### Coach Partner Program
Earn 20% recurring commission on every active client referral. Enroll free at startingmonday.app/partners. Receive a personalized referral link, coach-ready onboarding materials, and talking-point scripts. No minimum referral count required.

### Practice Model Paths
Choose the coach persona route that matches how you operate:
- **Independent coach (solo practice):** 1-15 clients, lightweight portfolio view, referral-only commission model
- **Boutique firm coach:** Team-based operations, shared client visibility settings, standardized scorecards
- **Enterprise-sponsored coach cohort:** Cohort governance, sponsor-ready reporting, program-level risk queue
- **Search-affiliate coach:** Search-adjacent workflow with trust boundary framing and interview arc integration

### 30-Day Pilot Scorecard
A structured pass/fail pilot framework for coaches evaluating the platform with 2-3 clients. Four metrics with clear success thresholds: session context-rebuild time, client preparation quality, between-session momentum, and client confidence trend. Decide from evidence at day 30, not from a sales pitch.

### Weekly Activity Digest
Each week, receive a summary of your client portfolio: who is active, who is drifting, new signals surfaced, and the one client most at risk of losing momentum. Keeps your oversight consistent without requiring daily manual review.

### Trust Pack
Detailed documentation for coaches who need to evaluate the platform for enterprise or firm-wide rollout: methodology notes, claims policy, pilot evidence with denominator disclosures, security posture, and legal review path. At startingmonday.app/for-coaches/trust-pack.

### Boutique Firm Tools (Boutique plan and above)
- Shared client views across the coaching team
- Standardized session scorecard template configurable per firm
- Coach performance benchmarking (aggregate, anonymized)
- Sponsor-ready cohort reporting for enterprise-sponsored programs

### Search-Affiliate Workflow
For coaches working alongside retained search firms: trust boundary documentation that satisfies search firm confidentiality requirements, interview arc visibility without compromising candidate-firm relationships, and a proof asset kit for demonstrating value to search firm partners.

## What coaches see vs. what clients control

| Feature | Client controls | Coach sees |
|---------|----------------|-----------|
| Target company list | Client adds/removes | Visible if client grants |
| Signal feed | Client's signals only | Read-only if granted |
| Pipeline status | Client manages | Summary view if granted |
| Prep briefs | Client generates | If client shares |
| Confidence scores | Auto-generated | Always visible to coach |
| Commitment tracker | Set in session | Visible to coach |

## Session workflow

| Touchpoint | What to do | Time required |
|-----------|------------|---------------|
| Day before session | Open pre-session snapshot | 5 min |
| Start of session | Reference what-changed summary | 2 min |
| During session | Log commitments in real time | 1-2 min |
| After session | Update next-action and risk markers | 2 min |
| Weekly | Review portfolio risk queue | 10 min |
