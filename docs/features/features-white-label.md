# Feature Guide: White-Label Partners

**What Starting Monday's white-label offering gives partner firms - one page**
**Platform entry:** Dashboard > Settings > White Label | Contact your account lead

---

## Core features

### Full Brand Customization
Your participants, counselors, and sponsors interact entirely with your brand. Configure:
- **Logo:** Appears on the dashboard, all emails, and exported reports
- **Brand name:** Replaces all "Starting Monday" references in the participant experience
- **Primary color:** Navigation, headers, and primary UI elements
- **Accent color:** CTAs, highlights, and key action buttons
- **Support email:** All participant support routing goes to your address

Changes apply instantly with a live preview before you save.

### Program Track Selection
Choose the participant journey that matches your program type:
- **Executive Transition:** VP and C-suite placements. Milestones: Intake complete, Prep ready, First outreach sent, Offer recorded.
- **Professional Transition:** Manager, director, and IC placements. Milestones: Intake complete, Positioning ready, First outreach sent, Interview completed.

Custom milestone sets available on Boutique and Outplacement tiers.

### Multi-Cohort Operations Hub (Outplacement tier)
Manage multiple simultaneous cohorts from one dashboard: active cohorts, enrollment status, milestone completion rates, counselor assignments, and sponsor report queue. Designed for national and enterprise programs running several cohort cycles per quarter.

### Cohort Dashboard and Participant Management
- Create named cohorts with custom start dates and program tracks
- Assign counselors to cohorts with configurable client-to-counselor ratios
- Track individual participant progress through program milestones
- Triage drifting or at-risk participants before they become escalations

### Sponsor Report Export (Boutique and Outplacement tiers)
Generate fully branded sponsor reports on demand:
- Cohort activation and utilization rates
- Milestone completion rates with methodology disclosure
- Confidence trend and engagement cadence
- KPI definitions and measurement notes
- Formatted for HR buyer, CFO, and board-level review

All reports use your brand name and logo. No Starting Monday attribution required unless you choose to include it.

### Governance Pack (Outplacement tier)
A complete governance documentation set for enterprise program management:
- SLA reporting templates
- Executive readout frameworks
- Cohort risk and intervention tracking
- Weekly operating review artifacts
- Procurement and legal review documentation

### Partner Role Management
Assign granular access levels to your team:
- **Firm admin:** Full settings access, billing, all cohorts, brand configuration
- **Program manager:** Cohort management, reporting, counselor oversight, no billing
- **Counselor:** Assigned participants only, session snapshots, no admin access

Add or revoke roles at **Dashboard > Settings > Team > Partner Roles**. Audit log available for enterprise accounts.

### White-Label Email System
All participant-facing emails are sent from your support address with your branding:
- Welcome and activation emails
- Daily briefing digest (your brand, your colors)
- Session reminder and commitment follow-ups
- Weekly activity summary
- Milestone completion notifications

Participants never see an email from Starting Monday.

### Priority Support (Boutique and Outplacement tiers)
Dedicated support response SLA: 4-hour response during business hours for Boutique, 2-hour for Outplacement. Includes a named account lead for quarterly business reviews and pilot planning.

### Participant Operating Features (included in all tiers)
Every participant in your white-labeled environment has access to the full participant feature set:
- Company intelligence scanner (48-hour monitoring cycle)
- Daily morning briefing
- Pipeline command center
- Prep brief generator
- Interview arc tracker
- Coach visibility controls (if your program includes coaching)
- What-changed session summaries

All delivered under your brand.

### Billing and Seat Management
- Overage seats are billed automatically at your tier's per-seat rate
- Add seats mid-month and pay prorated
- Upgrade tiers immediately; downgrade takes effect next billing cycle
- Usage dashboard shows seat consumption vs. included seats in real time

## What your participants experience

From signup through placement, participants see only your brand:
- Onboarding email from your address
- Dashboard with your logo and colors
- Prep briefs and reports with your firm name
- Support routing to your team
- No Starting Monday branding visible by default

---

## Getting started

White-label activation requires a Starting Monday account. If you are not yet set up, contact support@startingmonday.app or request access through your account lead. Basic configuration (branding, track, first cohort) is typically live within 1 business day.
