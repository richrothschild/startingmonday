# Role Expansion Beta Launch Checklist

## Scope

- Role-aware workflows for leadership, technical leadership, and delivery leadership lanes.
- Signal source governance and relationship targeting pipeline.
- Premium contact intelligence guardrails and policy consent capture.

## Engineering Readiness

- [ ] Role taxonomy and workflow routing migrations applied.
- [ ] Signal source catalog endpoint and governance summary are healthy.
- [ ] Relationship recommendations endpoint logs KPI-chain events.
- [ ] Low-confidence relationship review queue is enabled.
- [ ] Consent metadata is persisted for email and OAuth signups.
- [ ] Typecheck passes.
- [ ] Focused test suite passes.

## Legal Readiness

- [ ] Terms include outreach and contact-intelligence permitted-use language.
- [ ] Privacy policy includes role/signal/relationship data category disclosure.
- [ ] Policy versions are pinned in code.
- [ ] New users must accept terms and privacy before account creation.
- [ ] Policy acceptance metadata is persisted and queryable.

## GTM Readiness

- [ ] Role-lane tutorials are available in onboarding/start flow.
- [ ] Manager Tools bridge links and keyword prompts are visible.
- [ ] Recruiter-targeting messaging is ready for pilot outreach.
- [ ] KPI dashboard tracks signal -> relationship -> interview funnel.

## Go/No-Go Gate

- [ ] Engineering lead sign-off
- [ ] Legal lead sign-off
- [ ] GTM lead sign-off
- [ ] Product owner final approval
