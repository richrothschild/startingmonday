# Expansion Packet Template

## 1. Expansion Context
- Current partner footprint
- Requested expansion scope (cohorts, seats, support level)
- Named business goal for expansion

## 2. Day-60 Gate Review (Scale Readiness)
- Cohort activation trend (30-day and 60-day)
- Session strategy-time ratio trend
- Counselor variance trend by cohort
- Scale readiness recommendation

## 3. Day-90 Gate Review (Stability and Renewal Confidence)
- Program consistency trend across cohorts
- Intervention closure rate
- Sponsor-level KPI confidence statement
- Renewal probability estimate with rationale

## 4. Expansion Economics
- Incremental seat model
- Support model options
- Margin and service-level assumptions

## 5. Operating Commitments
- Governance cadence and owners
- Reporting obligations by month
- Escalation protocol and response windows

## 6. Contract Path
- Expansion option selected
- Effective date
- Signatures and approvals
