# Pilot Closeout Packet Template

## 1. Program Snapshot
- Partner name
- Cohort window
- Contract scope and seat mix
- Program owner and sponsor owner

## 2. Day-30 Decision Gate (Pass/Tune/Stop)
- Activation rate (target and actual)
- Prep-readiness rate (target and actual)
- Stall detection latency (target and actual)
- Decision outcome with rationale

## 3. Execution Review
- What improved versus baseline
- What remained unstable
- Intervention actions completed

## 4. Risk and Trust Review
- Open risk register with owner and ETA
- Claims and methodology references used in reporting
- Data quality limitations and confidence rating

## 5. Commercial Closeout Position
- Close without expansion
- Corrective 30-day extension
- Proceed to renewal packet

## 6. Sponsor Signoff
- Decision owner name
- Signed date
- Follow-up date for next checkpoint
