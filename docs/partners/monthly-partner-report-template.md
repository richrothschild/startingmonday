# Monthly Partner Report Template

## 1. Partner Summary
- Partner name
- Reporting month
- Accounts in scope
- Primary owner and operating contacts

## 2. KPI Snapshot
- Active campaigns
- Stalled campaigns (14+ days idle)
- Outreach starts
- Follow-up SLA performance
- Conversion progression (applied -> interviewing -> offer)

## 3. Trend and Variance Notes
- Week-over-week movement by KPI
- Notable wins and losses
- Known data quality limitations

## 4. Execution Quality Findings
- What improved this month
- What regressed this month
- Coach and operator observations

## 5. Risks and Interventions
- Risk register (owner, severity, ETA)
- Stalled campaign recovery plan
- Escalation items requiring partner decision

## 6. Next-Month Commitments
- Top 3 execution priorities
- Responsible owner per commitment
- Target check-in date
