# Renewal Decision Packet Template

## 1. Executive Summary
- Current contract period
- Primary outcomes delivered
- Confidence statement for renewal

## 2. Performance Evidence
- KPI trend appendix (monthly)
- Cohort-level outcomes
- Program stability indicators

## 3. Operational Review
- What worked repeatedly
- What failed and why
- Corrective actions completed

## 4. Renewal Options
- Option A: Continue current program scope
- Option B: Expand seats and support
- Option C: Narrow to high-fit segment

## 5. Commercial Terms
- Proposed pricing by option
- Service level and response assumptions
- Payment and term options

## 6. Decision Checklist
- Success criteria confirmed
- Risks accepted or mitigated
- Final approval owner and signature date
