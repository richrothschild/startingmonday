# Pre-Scale Weekly Operator Scorecard

Purpose: enforce the pre-scale checklist weekly so growth does not outpace proof, trust, and economics.

Cadence: every Friday
Owners: CEO, Revenue lead, Product and Engineering lead

## Gate logic

- Scale allowed: all metrics Green for two consecutive weeks.
- Scale caution: any Yellow persists two weeks.
- Scale blocked: any Red in proof, trust, or economics.

## Weekly review protocol

1. Update each metric with evidence.
2. Mark Green, Yellow, or Red.
3. Record one decision and one owner per non-Green metric.
4. Record due date for each decision.
5. Re-check prior week commitments.

## Non-negotiable escalation triggers

1. No named proof assets after two weeks: pause outbound scaling and prioritize proof sprint.
2. Search threat objections above 40% of discovery calls: hold search pilot expansion and revise trust framing.
3. Segment economics incomplete for two consecutive weeks: freeze hiring decisions.
4. Activation instrumentation incomplete in active segment: pause feature expansion requests.
