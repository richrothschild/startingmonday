# Coach Traction Sprint: 14 Days + 20 Outbound Messages

Date: 2026-05-22
Owner: Rich
Goal: Generate first coach design partners and first paid customers from executive coach channel

## Sprint Outcome Targets (14 Days)

1. 20 live conversations with qualified coaches.
2. 8 coach pilot starts (2 to 3 client seats each).
3. 3 written coach testimonials or quote approvals.
4. 2 paying coach accounts by day 14, or clear block reasons documented.

## Who To Target This Sprint

Primary target profile:

1. Independent executive coaches or 1 to 10 person firms.
2. Active on LinkedIn in the last 30 days.
3. Publicly discuss transitions, interview prep, VP-to-CXO moves, or board pathways.
4. Have at least one signal of active client demand now.

Do not target this sprint:

1. Broad leadership coaches with no transition focus.
2. Coaches who only serve early-career job seekers.
3. Profiles with no recent activity and no warm path.

## Daily Scoreboard

Track each day:

1. New connection requests sent.
2. Connection acceptance rate.
3. Replies received.
4. Calls booked.
5. Calls completed.
6. Pilot starts.
7. Paid conversions.
8. Top objection of the day.

## Decision Rules

1. If acceptance rate is below 35 percent after 40 requests, tighten targeting before changing copy.
2. If response rate is below 12 percent after 30 accepted connections, rewrite first follow-up message.
3. If call-to-pilot conversion is below 30 percent, improve pilot framing and proof assets.
4. If pilots start but do not convert, diagnose week-1 value delivery before scaling volume.
