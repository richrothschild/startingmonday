## The Pitch

At the VP and C-suite level, the best roles are filled before they're posted.

The candidate who knows first, arrives most prepared, and manages the process like a campaign wins. Starting Monday is the platform that makes that candidate you.

---

## What It Does

**Watches your target companies.** Career pages at your target organizations are scanned three times a week — before roles appear on LinkedIn or job boards. Company intelligence (funding rounds, executive hires and departures, acquisitions, expansions) is monitored and flagged the moment it surfaces.

**Prepares you in 60 seconds.** When a call is scheduled, open the company workspace and generate a prep brief. It is built from their recent signals, your resume, and any documents you've attached — not a generic company summary. A brief that knows both sides of the conversation.

**Runs the campaign.** Pipeline tracking, contact management, follow-up reminders, and a daily morning briefing replace the spreadsheet every executive builds and immediately falls behind on.

**An advisor that knows your situation.** The AI chat assistant has full context — your resume, your pipeline, recent signals — and can help you think through positioning, draft outreach, tailor your resume for a specific role, and take actions directly.

## Who It's For

Senior professionals running a targeted search: VP, SVP, CIO, CTO, COO, CFO. Executives who are actively searching, quietly monitoring, or running a confidential campaign while still employed.

**The search that Starting Monday is built for**: 15–30 target organizations. A small set of relationships that matter. A first call where the stakes are high and preparation is everything. A process that rewards discipline, not volume.

## Why Now

Executive search infrastructure has not changed in thirty years. Outplacement firms charge $10,000–$50,000 and take weeks to produce a generic brief. Job boards index roles that have already been filled. The spreadsheet-and-coffees approach depends entirely on who you know and whether you remembered to follow up.

Starting Monday is what the process should have been. Automated intelligence. Precise preparation. A campaign infrastructure that runs whether you are actively working it or not.
