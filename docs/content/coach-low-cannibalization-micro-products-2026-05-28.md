# Coach Micro Products With Low Cannibalization Risk

Date: 2026-05-28
Audience: executive coaches evaluating one-time products that should feed adoption of the Starting Monday app.

## Positioning rule

To avoid cannibalizing the app, every micro product should do one setup, one conversation, or one decision well.

The app should remain the place for:

1. multi-client visibility
1. ongoing readiness and momentum tracking
1. shared prep and accountability workflows
1. recurring operating rhythm between sessions

That means the safest micro products are static tools, templates, and guided artifacts that create value once, then make the buyer want a system that keeps the work running.

## Five low-cannibalization product ideas

## Which five are safest

Safest from a cannibalization standpoint:

1. Board and Stakeholder Update Writing Kit
1. Executive Positioning Narrative Builder
1. Executive Proof Library Builder
1. First 30 Days Transition Conversation Pack
1. Interview Debrief and Recovery Pack

Why these are safer than another scorecard or accountability tool:

- They create assets, not systems.
- They solve moments, not ongoing workflows.
- They increase appetite for the app's recurring value instead of duplicating it.
