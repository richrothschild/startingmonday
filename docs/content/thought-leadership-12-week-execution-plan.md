# 12-Week Thought Leadership Execution Plan

Date: 2026-05-22
Owner: Rich Rothschild
Brand focus: Starting Monday founder authority in executive-search readiness
Primary objective: Become a top-of-mind voice for senior executives, executive coaches, and search-adjacent advisors

## Strategic Intent

This plan is designed to increase authority in the right circles, not maximize vanity reach.

Own one core category phrase across all channels:

- Executive Search Readiness System

Own one signature framework:

- Executive Readiness Index (ERI): Signal Quality, Narrative Clarity, Pipeline Discipline, Interview Preparedness, Relationship Velocity

Every weekly output must do at least one of these:

- Increase credibility with evidence
- Increase recall with repeated category language
- Increase access through trusted-circle distribution

---

## Target Circles (Priority Order)

1. Executive coaches serving VP-to-CXO and C-suite transitions
2. Retained search partners and recruiter-adjacent advisors
3. Senior operator communities (CIO, CTO, CISO, CDO)
4. Board and governance pathway networks
5. Leadership media, podcast hosts, and conference organizers

## Weekly Output Formula (Exact)

Every week, publish and execute exactly:

1. One anchor insight memo (900-1,300 words)
2. Three founder posts (LinkedIn)
3. One proof artifact post (chart, mini-case, or denominator-backed snapshot)
4. Fifteen strategic comments in target circles
5. Twelve direct relationship touches (host, editor, coach, advisor, or community operator)
6. One live conversation (podcast recording, webinar, private roundtable, or council review)
7. One Friday scorecard review and adjustment memo

## 12-Week Sprint Plan

### Week 1: Foundation and message lock

Outputs:

- Finalize category phrase and ERI framework one-pager
- Publish anchor memo 1: What executive-search readiness actually means
- Publish three founder posts derived from memo 1
- Build initial target list of 75 names across five circles
- Execute 12 warm outreach messages

KPIs:

- 3 posts live
- 1 anchor memo live
- 12 outreach messages sent
- 15 strategic comments completed

### Week 2: Evidence baseline

Outputs:

- Publish proof artifact 1 with denominator, timeframe, and confidence note
- Publish anchor memo 2: The hidden readiness gap in senior searches
- Publish three founder posts
- Book one live conversation for week 3 or week 4

KPIs:

- 100% of claims in week content have denominator and date
- At least 1 qualified inbound from target circles

### Week 3: Coach-circle penetration

Outputs:

- Run one executive-coach roundtable (private, 45 minutes)
- Publish anchor memo 3: Between-session discipline and coaching outcomes
- Publish three founder posts and one proof artifact
- Send 12 follow-ups to coach and advisor nodes

KPIs:

- 8+ coach-circle replies
- 2+ invitations for follow-up conversation

### Week 4: Search-firm credibility

Outputs:

- Publish anchor memo 4: What retained search partners wish candidates tracked earlier
- Publish three founder posts

KPIs:

- 2+ earned appearance confirmations by end of week
- 3+ qualified inbound opportunities total this month

### Week 5: Signature framework launch

Outputs:

- Publish formal ERI v1 document (flagship asset)
- Publish three founder posts explaining ERI components
- Publish one mini-case applying ERI to a real scenario
- Conduct one live breakdown session of ERI with target audience

KPIs:

- 5+ organic references to ERI language by others

### Week 6: Multi-channel distribution burst

Outputs:

- Repurpose ERI into guest article pitch, webinar abstract, and podcast talking points
- Publish anchor memo 5 and three founder posts

### Week 8: Board and governance narrative

4. Do not add channels unless current channels are meeting green thresholds.
