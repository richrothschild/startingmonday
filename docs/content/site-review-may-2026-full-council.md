## Tensions and Resolutions

### Tension 1: Social proof demand vs trust discipline

- Conflict: growth/revenue members want stronger proof metrics; trust/legal members reject weakly verified claims.
- Resolution: publish a small verified metrics panel with update date and methodology note; avoid unverified counts.

### Tension 2: Strong urgency vs confidentiality calm

- Conflict: sales members push harder urgency; executive personas need psychological safety.
- Resolution: pair urgency and reassurance in same module (e.g., "window closes in days" plus "private by default").

### Tension 3: Growth loop visibility vs homepage simplicity

- Conflict: growth/BD members want immediate loop surfacing; design/brand members warn about clutter.
- Resolution: add exactly one loop CTA first (referral or partners), measure impact, then iterate.

### Tension 4: Persona tailoring vs low-friction onboarding

- Conflict: persona council wants branching; UX warns about choice overload.
- Resolution: keep one primary CTA, but personalize signup heading/first step from selected persona.
