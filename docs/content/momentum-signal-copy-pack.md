# Momentum Signal Copy Pack

This copy pack standardizes product language after replacing Executive Motion Intelligence (EMI) with Momentum Signal.

## 1) Dashboard card

- Label: Momentum Signal
- Value helper: Weekly executive search momentum
- Supporting line: A single score that shows whether your actions are creating real conversation movement.
- Status helper (healthy): Your Momentum Signal is rising. Keep cadence and narrative quality steady.
- Status helper (watch): Your Momentum Signal is flat. Tighten first-call prep and targeting this week.
- Status helper (risk): Your Momentum Signal is falling. Reset role narrative and weekly outreach quality now.

## 2) Empty-state language

- Title: Build your first Momentum Signal
- Body: Add one target company, one role narrative, and one outreach action. We will generate your first Momentum Signal after those actions are logged.
- CTA primary: Start my first Momentum Signal
- CTA secondary: See how Momentum Signal is calculated

## 3) Tooltip + help modal

### Tooltip (short)

Momentum Signal is a weekly indicator of whether your positioning and outreach execution are creating forward motion.

### Help modal (long)

- Title: What Momentum Signal means
- Definition: Momentum Signal combines narrative clarity, outreach quality, and progression activity into one weekly score.
- Why it matters: Executive searches stall when activity is high but quality is inconsistent. Momentum Signal shows whether your effort is translating into real movement.
- How to raise it:
  - Sharpen first-call narrative for your target role.
  - Improve outreach quality before increasing volume.
  - Log outcomes quickly so the next week reflects real feedback.
- Important note: Momentum Signal is directional guidance, not a guarantee of outcomes.

## 4) Weekly email summary

- Subject: Your weekly Momentum Signal update
- Preheader: See what moved, what stalled, and where to focus next week.
- Intro line: Your Momentum Signal this week is {{score}} ({{delta}} vs last week).
- Section heading 1: What improved your Momentum Signal
- Section heading 2: What is limiting momentum
- Section heading 3: One priority for next week
- Primary CTA: Review Momentum Signal details
- Secondary CTA: Start next week plan

- Sales deck headline: Momentum Signal turns executive search activity into measurable movement.
- Sales deck subhead: One weekly indicator of whether positioning and outreach quality are creating real forward motion.
- Site headline option A: Build Momentum Before the Market Sees You.
- Site headline option B: One Weekly Momentum Signal for Your Executive Search.
- Site headline option C: Stop Guessing. Start Tracking Momentum.
