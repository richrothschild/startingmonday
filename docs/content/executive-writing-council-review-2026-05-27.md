## 4) Required Changes to Reach 10% Opens and 3% Replies

### Cross-cutting changes (must apply to all posts)

1. Replace broad hooks with sharp pattern interrupts tied to coach pain.
2. Add one concrete number or mini-case in every post.
3. Shift CTA format from broad question to constrained choice.
4. Add friction-aware stakes language: cost of delay, missed mandate, lost candidate.
5. Use controlled controversy in at least 3/10 posts.
6. Strip passive phrasing and hedge-heavy clauses.
7. Keep one idea per post and compress line count by 10%-20%.

### CTA pattern that can realistically improve replies

Use one of these formats per post:

- Binary vote: "A or B?"
- Benchmark reveal: "Reply score and I will send the rubric."
- Field test call: "Reply test and I will send the 14-day script."

Rule:

- CTA must ask for one-word or one-number replies.

### Annual report rewrite requirements

1. Replace title with outcome-first headline focused on coach economics.
2. Add an opening section: "Three costly mistakes executive coaches are making with AI right now."
3. Move source-method section to appendix or final third.
4. Convert recommendations into a 30-60-90 operator checklist.
5. Add one contrarian claim and defend it with bounded evidence.
6. Add a one-question discussion prompt to generate comments when shared.
