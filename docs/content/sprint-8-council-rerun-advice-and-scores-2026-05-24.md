## Cross-council advice summary

Why they would buy overall:
- Buyer-specific routes, proof stack sequencing, and trust artifacts now align better to real procurement and coaching workflows.
- Onboarding and execution mechanics are materially more usable for high-stress personas.
- Governance cadence provides confidence that quality and claims discipline can be sustained.

Why they would not buy overall:
- If message focus drifts back to umbrella positioning, perceived relevance will decline.
- If evidence and instrumentation cadence weakens, trust in outcomes and economics will degrade.
- If friction returns in onboarding or coach workflows, retention and conversion gains will reverse.
