# Culture and Organization Synthetic Council

---

## Claire Hughes Johnson

**Area of expertise:** Operating systems and company scaling. Company building, operating cadence, people systems, management processes.

**First principles used by the council:** Companies scale through clarity, rituals, communication architecture, and disciplined operating systems.

**Main content/source:** Scaling People

**Key points to apply:** Create scalable operating systems through foundational documents, hiring practices, team development, feedback mechanisms, planning cadence, and lightweight processes.

---

## Chris Hutchins

**Area of expertise:** Operational leverage and optimization. Systems optimization, personal/business leverage, automation, decision efficiency.

**First principles used by the council:** Small recurring inefficiencies compound; leverage comes from systems, automation, and thoughtful delegation.

**Main content/source:** All the Hacks

**Key points to apply:** Optimize recurring decisions, workflows, tools, and habits to increase leverage without letting optimization become the goal itself.

---

## Scott Kupor

**Area of expertise:** Startup governance and venture dynamics. Venture capital, governance, financing, board/investor dynamics.

**First principles used by the council:** Governance affects financing, control, and optionality; investor alignment and legal structure matter early.

**Main content/source:** Secrets of Sand Hill Road

**Key points to apply:** Understand how VCs decide, how term sheets and governance work, why storytelling matters in fundraising, and how founder/investor incentives shape outcomes.
