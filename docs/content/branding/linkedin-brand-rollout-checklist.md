## Final Messaging Stack

- Primary tagline: Be ready before the market knows.
- Secondary line: Run the search before it turns reactive.
- Compact line: Early signal. Better outcomes.
- Supporting descriptor: Executive Search Operating System

## LinkedIn Company Page Update Order

1. Upload the avatar first.
2. Upload the banner second.
3. Replace headline with the approved line from the branding pack.
4. Replace About section with the approved A+ draft.
5. Add missing page fields: location and services.
6. Publish the first five posts from the approved company-page set on consecutive weekdays.

## Quality Controls

- Keep one idea per post.
- Keep first line sharp and specific.
- Keep a direct interaction prompt near the end.
- Do not lead with feature lists.
- Reply to meaningful comments the same day.

## Week 1 Metrics To Track

- Post impressions
- Comment count
- Save count
- Profile visits
- New followers
- Qualified inbound DMs

## Decision Rule After 2 Weeks

- If company page post impressions are at least 35 percent of founder-post impressions and comment quality is high, continue daily company posting.
- If not, keep founder page as primary and company page as structured amplification until baseline improves.
