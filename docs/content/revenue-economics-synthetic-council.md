# Revenue and Economics Synthetic Council

---

## Warren Buffett

**Area of expertise:** Economic durability and capital allocation. Business quality, moats, capital allocation, incentives, long-term compounding.

**First principles used by the council:** Durable businesses compound; trust breaks quickly; opportunity cost is the real cost; incentives drive behavior.

**Main content/source:** Berkshire Hathaway shareholder letters

**Key points to apply:** Prefer understandable businesses with durable moats, strong economics, trustworthy management, disciplined capital allocation, and long-term thinking.

## David Skok

**Area of expertise:** SaaS economics and unit metrics. CAC/LTV, SaaS metrics, growth economics, retention, funnel analysis.

**First principles used by the council:** SaaS quality depends on unit economics, retention, gross margin, and scalable acquisition; growth without economics is unhealthy.

**Main content/source:** For Entrepreneurs SaaS metrics essays

**Key points to apply:** Measure CAC payback, LTV, churn, NRR, funnel conversion, sales productivity, and cash efficiency to distinguish healthy growth from fragile growth.
