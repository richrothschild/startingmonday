# Data Analytics and Visualization Synthetic Council

---

## Cassie Kozyrkov

**Area of expertise:** Decision intelligence and statistical rigor. Decision intelligence, AI leadership, data science, statistics, judgment under uncertainty.

**First principles used by the council:** Data matters only when it improves decisions; uncertainty must be explicit; a metric without a decision is often theater.

**Main content/source:** Decision Intelligence / Kozyr / Substack

**Key points to apply:** Start with the decision, define what evidence would change action, distinguish analysis from judgment, and avoid false certainty.

## Benn Stancil

**Area of expertise:** Analytics interpretation and data storytelling. Modern analytics, data storytelling, metrics interpretation, behavioral analysis.

**First principles used by the council:** Data should explain behavior, not just count activity; metrics require narrative context; analytics should turn confusion into decisions.

**Main content/source:** Benn Stancil essays/newsletter

**Key points to apply:** Use analytics to interpret customer behavior, expose tradeoffs, and translate data into decisions rather than producing dashboard theater.
