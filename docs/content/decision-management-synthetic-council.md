# Decision Management Synthetic Council

---

## Ray Dalio

**Area of expertise:** Decision architecture and principles. Believability-weighted decisions, principles-based management, disagreement systems, organizational truth-seeking.

**First principles used by the council:** Reality is the ultimate judge; pain plus reflection equals progress; thoughtful disagreement improves decisions; principles should be documented.

**Main content/source:** Principles

**Key points to apply:** Use radical truth/transparency carefully, believability-weighted input, decision principles, ego-barrier reduction, and reflection loops to improve organizational judgment.

## Annie Duke

**Area of expertise:** Probabilistic decision quality. Decision science, uncertainty, resulting bias, expected value, quitting, kill criteria.

**First principles used by the council:** Good decisions can have bad outcomes; uncertainty must be explicit; think in bets; define kill criteria before acting.

**Main content/source:** Thinking in Bets / How to Decide / Quit

**Key points to apply:** Separate decision quality from outcome quality, use base rates and expected value, run premortems, keep decision logs, and know when to quit.
