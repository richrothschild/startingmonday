# Micro Products Creation and Sales Plan

Date: 2026-05-27
Owner: Starting Monday
Primary objective: generate near-term revenue while increasing authority, trust, and top-of-funnel awareness for core platform sales.

## Strategy in one line

Use low-ticket, high-clarity micro products as trust accelerators that solve one painful decision at a time for three buying lanes:

1. Executive coaches
1. Senior executives in transition
1. Outplacement and transition firms

## Success model

Micro products should do four jobs simultaneously:

1. Produce direct cash flow.
1. Create proof of expertise with practical, outcome-linked artifacts.
1. Feed qualified buyers into higher-ticket offers.
1. Build recurring content and social credibility loops.

## Product architecture

Offer stack should be structured in three tiers:

1. Entry: impulse and fast utility ($19 to $79).
1. Core: practical operating systems ($99 to $399).
1. Team: implementation kits for firms ($499 to $1,999).

## Recommended first 8 micro products

## 1) Executive Search Readiness Scorecard

- Audience: executives
- Price: $39
- Format: downloadable scorecard + interpretation guide
- Promise: identify where your search process is leaking momentum in 20 minutes
- Upsell path: annual report bundle, coaching operating kit, platform trial

## 2) Coach Session Anti-Drift Kit

- Audience: executive coaches
- Price: $79
- Format: templates, pre-session brief, session close protocol, weekly review sheet
- Promise: reduce context rebuild and increase between-session execution
- Upsell path: coach operating system subscription

## 3) Shadow Search Governance Pack

- Audience: boards, search advisors, executive coaches
- Price: $99
- Format: governance checklist, communication protocol, risk memo templates
- Promise: reduce governance breakdown risk before confidential search motions
- Upsell path: advisory workshop and premium implementation package

## 4) 30-60-90 Transition Brief Builder

- Audience: executives and outplacement professionals
- Price: $59
- Format: worksheet + generated narrative structure + examples
- Promise: produce a board-ready transition narrative quickly
- Upsell path: positioning deep-dive service

## 5) Outplacement Execution Quality Audit Sheet

- Audience: outplacement firms
- Price: $149
- Format: audit rubric, KPI schema, intervention triggers
- Promise: show clients measurable transition support quality, not activity theater
- Upsell path: team enablement package

## 6) Executive Networking Conversion Playbook

- Audience: executives
- Price: $49
- Format: scripts, role-based outreach map, follow-up rhythm tracker
- Promise: convert warm contacts into real conversations faster
- Upsell path: platform and briefing workflows

## 7) Recruiter Brief Quality Rubric

- Audience: search firms and coaches
- Price: $89
- Format: rubric, scoring examples, rewrite framework
- Promise: improve candidate and role fit quality before outbound begins
- Upsell path: search-firm operating kit

## 8) Weekly Decision Cadence Board

- Audience: all three lanes
- Price: $29
- Format: Notion and PDF versions with weekly prompts
- Promise: create decision rhythm under uncertainty
- Upsell path: recurring subscription and monthly advisory call

## 90-day build and go-to-market plan

## Phase 1 (Days 1-14): Validation and offer shaping

1. Select first three products:
   - Executive Search Readiness Scorecard
   - Coach Session Anti-Drift Kit
   - Outplacement Execution Quality Audit Sheet
1. Run ten customer interviews across each segment.
1. Validate painful decision and willingness to pay using one-page concept tests.
1. Define exact outcomes and minimum acceptable proof for each product.

Exit criteria:

1. At least 60 percent positive purchase intent across interviews.
1. At least five pre-orders or paid pilots across the first three offers.

## Phase 2 (Days 15-35): Build MVP assets

1. Build v1 of all three products in simple formats:
   - PDF
   - editable worksheet
   - optional Notion companion
1. Create purchase pages with clear promise, target user, and one concrete use case.
1. Add delivery automation:
   - checkout
   - instant download
   - follow-up sequence
1. Create support SOP:
   - FAQs
   - refund policy
   - versioning process

Exit criteria:

1. All assets fulfill in under 5 minutes from purchase.
1. At least three customer walkthroughs completed for quality feedback.

## Phase 3 (Days 36-60): Launch and demand capture

1. Publish launch sequence on LinkedIn and newsletter.
1. Use one product-focused post per audience each week.
1. Run one live workshop demo for each lane.
1. Capture testimonials and before/after examples.
1. Launch bundle pricing:
   - single product
   - two-product bundle
   - all-access bundle

Exit criteria:

1. 30 paid transactions total.
1. At least 20 percent of buyers open at least one upsell CTA.

## Phase 4 (Days 61-90): Scale and systematize

1. Optimize based on refund reasons and support questions.
1. Add second-wave products from the remaining list.
1. Introduce subscription or membership option for monthly updates.
1. Build affiliate or referral loop with coaches and outplacement partners.
1. Standardize monthly performance review and council governance cycle.

Exit criteria:

1. Product gross margin above 80 percent.
1. Repeat purchase rate above 15 percent.
1. Qualified lead flow into core platform offer increasing month over month.

## Pricing and packaging model

## Price ladder

1. Entry utility: $19 to $79
1. Operator kits: $99 to $399
1. Team packs: $499+

## Bundle strategy

1. Executive Transition Starter Bundle
1. Coach Execution Bundle
1. Outplacement Credibility Bundle

## Offer mechanics

1. Fast-start guarantee: outcome in 30 minutes or less.
1. Proof block on every page: concrete operator outcomes.
1. Direct CTA path into core product or advisory call.

## Sales channels

Primary channels:

1. LinkedIn founder profile and company page posts
1. Email list drops and segmented nurture sequences
1. Partner distribution through coaches and outplacement firms

Secondary channels:

1. Live cohort-style demos
1. Podcast and webinar guest appearances
1. Executive communities and alumni networks

## Content and awareness engine

Use a repeating content loop:

1. Pain point post
1. Contrarian framing post
1. Practical checklist post
1. Product call-to-action post

Each micro product should generate:

1. One long-form article
1. Three short social posts
1. One webinar/demo script
1. One case-study style follow-up post

## Operational stack

Minimum required systems:

1. Checkout and fulfillment workflow
1. CRM tags by segment and purchased product
1. Email automation by product and audience
1. Analytics dashboard for conversion, refunds, and upsells
1. Product feedback intake and release notes

## KPIs and guardrails

Top KPIs:

1. conversion rate by product page
1. average order value
1. refund rate
1. repeat purchase rate
1. upsell conversion into higher-ticket offers

Guardrails:

1. Keep refund rate below 7 percent.
1. Keep support burden below 15 minutes per sale on average.
1. Avoid custom work requests that erode product margin.

## Risks and mitigations

Risk: content demand rises faster than delivery quality

Mitigation:

1. monthly quality council pass required before launch
1. limited launch cohort before broad release

Risk: products become generic templates

Mitigation:

1. include lane-specific examples and edge cases
1. quarterly update cycle with customer evidence

Risk: low-ticket products fail to convert upstream

Mitigation:

1. embed one strategic upsell moment in each product
1. map each product to one specific next offer
