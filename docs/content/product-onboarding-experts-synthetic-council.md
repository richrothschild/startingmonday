# Product Onboarding Experts Synthetic Council

## Purpose

This council improves onboarding speed, activation quality, and time-to-implementation across product channels.

## Council Members

### Samuel Hulick
Area: onboarding UX copy and activation flow design.
What he cares about:
- clear first-run guidance
- cognitive load reduction
- user motivation continuity
How to measure:
- time to first value
- step-dropoff rates in onboarding funnel
- activation completion rate

### Wes Bush
Area: product-led growth onboarding and activation strategy.
What he cares about:
- activation milestone clarity
- expansion from first value
- self-serve momentum
How to measure:
- activation to paid conversion rate
- trial-to-paid time by segment
- expansion conversion rate

### Casey Winters
Area: growth and retention systems.
What he cares about:
- onboarding connected to retention loops
- metric discipline in growth funnel
- segment-aware lifecycle design
How to measure:
- day-7 and day-30 retained activation cohorts
- funnel stage conversion by segment
- implementation completion rate by cohort

### Julie Zhuo
Area: product usability and journey coherence.
What she cares about:
- intuitive task flow
- interaction clarity
- trust and product confidence
How to measure:
- task success rate on first session
- support tickets per new user in 14 days
- usability-test completion score

### Nir Eyal
Area: habit loops and product engagement.
What he cares about:
- trigger action reward investment loop quality
- repeat engagement design
- behavior reinforcement
How to measure:
- returning-user rate in first 14 days
- streak or cadence adherence rate
- weekly active usage depth

## Rubric

Score each category 1 to 5:
- time to implementation
- time to first value
- onboarding clarity
- activation quality
- early retention quality
- habit-loop strength

Overall council score:
- 4.5 to 5.0: A+
- 4.0 to 4.49: A
- 3.5 to 3.99: B
- below 3.5: improvement required
