# Newsletter Growth Operating System (Starting Monday)

Audience: executive coaches.
Cadence: weekly.
Horizon: 12 weeks.

## Strategic Focus Decision

Primary recommendation: focus on executive coaches for the next 12 weeks.

Rationale:

- Faster trust loops and feedback cycles than broad job-search audiences.
- Higher downstream leverage because each coach influences multiple executive clients.
- Better fit for Starting Monday positioning around between-session execution discipline.

Secondary track: keep job-search content as a repurposing lane only.

- Target split: 70 percent executive coach content, 30 percent executive job-search derivative content.

Decision rule after 12 weeks:

- Stay coach-first if coach-sourced pipeline contribution is at least 2x job-search sourced contribution.
- Rebalance if coach-sourced contribution is below 1.5x for four consecutive weeks.

## North Star And Guardrails

North star metric:

- Qualified conversations per week from newsletter audience.

Guardrails:

- Unsubscribe rate below 0.4 percent weekly.
- Spam complaint rate below 0.08 percent.
- No week with newsletter send skipped.

## Weekly Experiment Quota

Minimum weekly experiments:

- 2 acquisition experiments.
- 1 conversion experiment.
- 1 retention experiment.
- 1 referral experiment.

Experiment quality bar:

- Each experiment must have a hypothesis, success metric, stop condition, and owner.

## Experiment Backlog (Start Here)

Acquisition:

- LinkedIn carousel teaser with artifact CTA versus plain text CTA.
- Guest swap with one coach newsletter and one search-firm newsletter.
- Directory listing refresh with tighter value proposition copy.

Conversion:

- Landing page hero test: pain-first headline versus outcome-first headline.
- Single-step versus two-step opt-in form.

Retention:

- Fixed four-part issue template versus variable format.
- One-question reply CTA versus resource download CTA.

Referral:

- Referral ladder A: checklist at 3, scorecard at 10.
- Referral ladder B: checklist at 2, office-hours invite at 8.

## Ownership Model

Founder:

- Final editorial signoff.
- CTA strategy.
- Reply handling and high-intent conversion.

Growth owner:

- Acquisition channels.
- Landing pages.
- Referral loops.

Lifecycle owner:

- Deliverability.
- Send operations.
- Segment health and retention.

Content owner:

- Issue production.
- Artifact quality.
- Content repurposing.

Revenue owner:

- Pipeline attribution.
- Offer testing.
- Conversion reporting.

## Weekly Operating Cadence

Monday:

- Review scoreboard and diagnose green/yellow/red.
- Lock this week experiments and owners.

Tuesday:

- Draft issue and build one practical artifact.
- Run council review and final approver pass.

Wednesday:

- Publish issue.
- Monitor first 6-hour performance and reply flow.

Thursday:

- Run conversion and referral follow-up actions.
- Push derivative social content to drive late-week opt-ins.

Friday:

- Retrospective.
- Archive learnings, update backlog, and lock next week hypotheses.

## 12-Week Targets (Coach-First)

Week 1 to 4:

- Establish baseline metrics.
- Reach 500 engaged subscribers.
- Achieve 2.5 percent click rate and 0.8 percent reply rate.

Week 5 to 8:

- Reach 1,200 engaged subscribers.
- Achieve 4.0 percent click rate and 1.2 percent reply rate.
- Reach 15 percent referral share.

Week 9 to 12:

- Reach 2,000 engaged subscribers.
- Achieve 4.5 percent click rate and 1.5 percent reply rate.
- Reach 20 percent referral share.
- Generate at least 6 qualified conversations per week.

## Segment Strategy (Coach-First, Job-Search Secondary)

Primary segment:

- Executive coaches in transition, VP-to-CXO, and search-adjacent practices.

Secondary segment:

- Senior executive job-search audience consuming repurposed insights.

Content allocation:

- 70 percent coach-operator content.
- 30 percent job-search derivative content.

## Go/No-Go Rules

Continue coach-first if all conditions hold:

- Coach-sourced pipeline remains at least 2x job-search pipeline.
- Unsubscribe and complaint rates stay in green/yellow.
- Qualified conversations trend flat or up over 4 weeks.

Rebalance strategy if either condition triggers:

- Coach-sourced pipeline below 1.5x job-search for 4 weeks.
- Activation rate below 35 percent for 3 consecutive weeks.
