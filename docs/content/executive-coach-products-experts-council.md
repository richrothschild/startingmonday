# Executive Coach Products Experts Council

## Purpose

This council evaluates products built for executive coaches, including utility between sessions, client outcomes, and business model viability for coaching practices.

## Council Members

### Michael Bungay Stanier
Area: coaching behavior design and scalable coaching methods.
What he cares about:
- practical coaching workflows
- behavior change between sessions
- coach adoption simplicity
How to measure:
- weekly active coach usage
- between-session action completion rate
- coach-reported session effectiveness lift

### Marshall Goldsmith
Area: executive behavior change and measurable coaching outcomes.
What he cares about:
- visible behavior change
- stakeholder-observed progress
- accountability cadence
How to measure:
- behavior-goal attainment rate
- stakeholder feedback delta over 60 to 90 days
- coaching engagement renewal rate

### David Peterson
Area: large-scale executive coaching systems.
What he cares about:
- evidence-based coaching support
- enterprise coaching fit
- consistency across clients and coaches
How to measure:
- standardized coaching plan adoption rate
- time to first meaningful coaching milestone
- enterprise partner expansion rate

### Jerry Colonna
Area: leadership development depth and trust.
What he cares about:
- human depth with operational rigor
- psychological safety in coaching workflows
- durable leader outcomes
How to measure:
- client confidence trend score
- coaching continuity over 6 plus months
- qualitative trust indicators in feedback

### Rich Litvin
Area: premium coaching offer design and executive buyer value.
What he cares about:
- premium perceived value
- clarity of transformation promise
- coach business leverage
How to measure:
- premium-tier conversion rate
- average revenue per coach account
- referral rate from coached executives

## Rubric

Score each category 1 to 5:
- coach workflow fit
- client behavior change support
- measurable coaching outcomes
- trust and psychological safety
- coach business economics
- enterprise scalability

Overall council score:
- 4.5 to 5.0: A+
- 4.0 to 4.49: A
- 3.5 to 3.99: B
- below 3.5: improvement required
