## Claim set covered

These are the claims this note is meant to support:

- 27 pilot executives in the Jan-May 2026 cohort
- 81% reached a first interview within 30 days
- 9-day median from setup to first outreach activity

## Time window

- Cohort start: January 2026
- Cohort end for this measurement window: May 2026

## Population definition

- Included: pilot executives who completed product setup and entered an active or near-active executive transition workflow during the Jan-May 2026 period
- Excluded: passive observers, incomplete setups, and accounts without enough data to determine outreach or interview status

## Metric definitions

### Pilot executives

- Count only unique executive users included in the Jan-May 2026 pilot cohort

### First interview within 30 days

- Definition: the user recorded or confirmed a first interview conversation within 30 calendar days of qualifying setup
- Current reported figure: 81%
- Current reported count basis: 27 executives

### Median time to first outreach

- Definition: median number of days from qualifying setup to first recorded outreach activity
- Current reported figure: 9 days

## Caveats

- This note supports a historical cohort claim, not a forward-looking promise
