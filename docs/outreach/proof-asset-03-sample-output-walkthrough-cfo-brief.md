# Proof Asset 03: Sample Output Walkthrough - CFO Search Brief

Purpose: show the type of artifact Starting Monday can generate so buyers and partners can evaluate substance, not just claims.

Source template: docs/outreach/sample-cfo-brief-template.md
Status: usable now as sample output
Confidentiality level: safe for sharing because the example is fictionalized

## Asset summary

This sample demonstrates the briefing layer Starting Monday can provide before a high-stakes conversation.

It is not a generic company summary. It is a decision artifact structured around the actual mandate, likely evaluation criteria, market context, and interview preparation.

## What the sample includes

1. Recent comparable leadership moves
2. Competitive context for the mandate
3. Candidate positioning angles
4. Likely candidate questions and grounded answers
5. Market intelligence and sponsor context
6. Interview prep prompts, green flags, and red flags
7. Offer-positioning and timing guidance

## Decision value created

For search firms:

- reduces partner re-briefing time
- improves kickoff quality
- gives consultants a reusable readiness artifact before candidate conversations

For coaches:

- sharpens session preparation
- keeps client conversations focused on strategy rather than rebuilding context

For executives:

- improves specificity before interviews
- shortens the distance between raw research and a credible executive-level conversation

## Why this counts as proof

Councils repeatedly flagged that Starting Monday needed to show, not just tell.

This asset answers that requirement by making the output legible:

- the artifact is structured
- the context is specific
- the questions are tied to the mandate
- the output is immediately usable by a human operator

## How to use this asset

1. In landing pages: link to the sample or excerpt key sections
2. In partner outreach: offer it as the first low-risk proof step
3. In demos: walk through it in under 3 minutes and explain the decisions it supports

## Required framing when sharing

- This is a sample output, not a promise of outcome.
