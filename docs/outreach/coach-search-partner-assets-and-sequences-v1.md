# Coach and Search Partner Assets and Sequences V1

Purpose: operationalize partner-channel motions for executive coaches and boutique retained search firms.

## Shared positioning guardrails

- Do not position as job-search automation.
- Do position as executive readiness and signal intelligence for better high-stakes conversations.
- Do not imply replacement of coach or search judgment.
- Do highlight leverage, preparation quality, and cadence discipline.

## Coach channel motion

### Primary offer

- Coach Partner Preview
- Components:
  - free coach access
  - 2-3 client preview seats
  - sample executive intelligence brief
  - private feedback session
  - optional referral economics

### Persona-specific value lines

1. Career transition coaches: less between-session chaos, faster client momentum.

1. VP-to-CXO coaches: clearer executive narrative and more consistent weekly execution.

1. Search-affiliate coaches: stronger pre-interview preparedness and cleaner client positioning.

1. Board-positioning coaches: disciplined opportunity monitoring and relationship continuity.

### Outreach sequence (coach)

Day 0

- Connection note with one real personalization signal.

Day 0-1

- Short follow-up: identify the between-session execution gap and offer 15-minute preview.

Day 3

- Check-in with optional 5-minute demo clip.

Day 7

- Final value-forward touch with no-pressure close.

Success thresholds:

- Acceptance: 40-60%
- Reply: 15-20%
- Meeting to pilot: 20-35%

## Search-firm channel motion

### Search primary offer

- Search Intelligence Partner Pilot
- Components:
  - private demo
  - sample readiness brief
  - sample company signal report
  - no-poach and confidentiality framing
  - trust and workflow feedback session

### Trust boundary statement (required in every search outreach asset)

- Not a sourcing replacement.
- Not a retained-search replacement.
- Not a CRM replacement.
- A readiness and signal layer that improves kickoff and candidate conversations.

### Outreach sequence (search)

Day 0

- Personalized intro referencing mandate pressure or consultant prep burden.

Day 1

- Follow-up with one sample brief excerpt and trust-boundary line.

Day 4

- Calibrated check-in question: where does prep rework hurt most today?

Day 8

- Final touch offering a pilot on two mandates.

Success thresholds:

- Reply: greater than 15%
- Meeting conversion: greater than 5%
- Qualified pilots: at least 3 from first 40 targets

## Required partner assets

1. Coach partner one-pager
2. Search partner one-pager
3. Objection sheet (coach)
4. Objection sheet (search)
5. Sample outputs pack
6. Pilot terms summary

## Objection bank

Coach objection: this replaces my value

- Response: Starting Monday runs execution context so sessions stay focused on strategy, narrative, and behavior change.

Coach objection: my clients can use LinkedIn

- Response: LinkedIn is broad visibility. Starting Monday adds structured signal monitoring and preparation cadence.

Search objection: we already do this research

- Response: this does not replace your research. It reduces re-briefing overhead and improves consultant readiness cadence.

Search objection: this could compete with us

- Response: it does not source candidates for your mandate and does not replace retained-search judgment.

## 30-day partner test plan

Week 1

- Finalize one-pagers and sample outputs.

Week 2

- Contact 20 coaches and 20 search consultants.

Week 3

- Run discovery calls and track objection patterns.

Week 4

- Decide go/no-go based on conversion, threat perception, and pilot willingness.
