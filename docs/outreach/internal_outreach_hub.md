## Weekly Operating Cadence

- Monday: select 10 prospects from the master 50 and send first-touch emails.
- Wednesday: send follow-up 1 to non-responders at day 3.
- Friday: send follow-up 2 to remaining non-responders at day 7 and review response quality.
- Friday: log status updates in your tracking sheet and refresh next-week send list.

## Suggested Status Values

Use one of these in your working tracker:

- `new`
- `first_sent`
- `followup_1_sent`
- `followup_2_sent`
- `replied`
- `meeting_booked`
- `not_a_fit`

## Quick Process

1. Copy 10 rows from the master 50 into your active send sheet.
2. Send first-touch notes from `send_ready_emails_first_10.csv` style.
3. Queue follow-ups from `send_ready_followups_first_10.csv` based on delay days.
4. Record outcomes and improve angle quality from real replies.
