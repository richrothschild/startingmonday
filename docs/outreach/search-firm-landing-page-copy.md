## FAQ SECTION

### How quickly can you deliver a brief?
24-48 hours from mandate details. Rush delivery (12 hours) available.

### What if we already have internal research capabilities?
This isn't a replacement for your research team. It's leverage for your consultants. You get pre-kickoff context so your team isn't starting from scratch.

### How do we actually use this?
We deliver the brief as a PDF or email. Forward it to your team before kickoff. They use it to prep their kickoff. No new software. No logins.

### Does this work for all role levels?
We focus on C-suite and senior VP roles (the lanes where market context matters most). CFO, COO, CIO, CHRO, CRO, CTO, CISO, CDO, and related VP roles.

### Can you customize by region or industry?
Yes. Tell us your mandate scope (US mid-market tech CFOs, for example). We tailor research accordingly.

### What if the brief isn't what we expected?
We refine on iteration 1. No penalty. Let's get it right for your practice.
