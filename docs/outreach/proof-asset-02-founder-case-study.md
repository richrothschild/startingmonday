# Proof Asset 02: Founder Operator Case Study

Purpose: provide an immediate, defensible case narrative grounded in the founder's real use case until more named external customer stories are collected.

Status: usable now
Owner: CEO
Confidentiality level: public if founder approves

## Profile

- Persona: Transformation CIO in active executive search
- Search context: senior technology leadership transition
- Environment: multiple target companies, high research burden, relationship-heavy process

## Starting challenge

Before Starting Monday, the search process depended on spreadsheets, notes, fragmented contact tracking, and repeated manual research before every conversation.

The failure mode was not lack of effort. It was loss of continuity. Context had to be rebuilt constantly, signals were easy to miss, and preparation quality depended on how much time could be clawed back each week.

## What changed

Starting Monday was built to replace that fragmented operating model with one system for:

- target-company monitoring
- signal tracking
- prep-brief generation
- outreach and follow-up discipline
- company-by-company conversation readiness

## Operational result

The founder no longer had to rebuild the same search context before every call.

Instead, the workflow became:

- monitor target companies continuously
- review signal and company updates in one place
- prepare from a structured brief rather than ad hoc research
- maintain search discipline across multiple companies at once

## Why this matters as proof

This case is not generic founder storytelling. It shows the product was built inside the exact workflow it is meant to improve.

That gives Starting Monday three defensible advantages:

1. The product reflects an executive-search operating problem, not a generic career-content problem.
2. The workflow is based on actual executive search behavior under pressure.
3. The product language and priorities come from a user who is also the builder.

## Approved quote candidate

- I built Starting Monday because the manual search process was unsustainable. I needed one system that could monitor companies, keep context intact, and let me prepare for every conversation without starting from zero.
