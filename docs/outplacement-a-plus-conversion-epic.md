# Outplacement A+ Conversion Epic

## Objective
Achieve A+ readiness across buyer conversion, operational execution, counselor adoption, and procurement governance for the outplacement partner motion.

## Primary KPI
Pilot-to-scale conversion rate for outplacement cohorts.

## Secondary KPIs
- Week-1 activation rate
- Day-30 pass-rate
- Session-yield improvement
- Procurement cycle time

## Sprint 1: Board-Approval Commercial Clarity
Goal: Move from planning-grade to decision-grade commercial framing.

Deliverables:
- Commercial example table by cohort size, seat mix, and term assumptions.
- Conservative placement-cycle business impact framing.
- Methodology panel with numerator, denominator, and measurement windows.
- Committee one-pager for procurement circulation.

Success criteria:
- Buyer can answer total-cost and decision-scope questions without follow-up deck.
- Method definitions are visible in-page and linked in trust artifacts.

## Sprint 2: Comparative Positioning and Governance
Goal: Make differentiation and governance explicit.

Deliverables:
- Current-model vs operating-layer comparison block.
- Governance role matrix with named meeting cadence and required outputs.
- Decision instrument language and clean-no branch at CTA.
- Calibrated decision questions in final sections.

Success criteria:
- Partner sponsor can identify owner, cadence, and output for every review meeting.
- Day-30 scale/tune/stop decision path is explicit and low-friction.

## Sprint 3: Operator-Ready Pilot Execution
Goal: Convert conceptual workflow into verbatim operational assets.

Deliverables:
- Downloadable pilot runbook with week-by-week tasks.
- Escalation rules with trigger thresholds and interventions.
- Friday MBR-lite packet template with metric definitions.
- Participant kickoff communication template.

Success criteria:
- Program operations lead can run pilot from templates without custom docs.
- Intervention ownership and thresholds are clear in first operating review.

## Sprint 4: Counselor Adoption and Session Yield
Goal: Reduce counselor resistance and increase strategic session quality.

Deliverables:
- Counselor enablement kit with first-session script pack.
- What-changed prep template for pre-session workflow.
- Red flags and intervention quick guide.
- Session-yield metrics (strategy-time vs context rebuild) and anonymized peer quotes.

Success criteria:
- Counselors can explain platform value without replacement anxiety.
- Session-yield metrics can be measured weekly and reviewed biweekly.

## Sprint 5: Legal, Claims, and Confidentiality Confidence
Goal: Make procurement and legal pathways board-safe.

Deliverables:
- Legal/procurement brief with role and data boundaries.
- Board-safe claims policy for partner and employer reporting.
- Staged KPI model for day 30, day 60, day 90.
- Confidentiality-by-design narrative and trust artifact index with request flow.

Success criteria:
- Legal and procurement teams can complete review using published trust artifacts.
- Partner-facing claims can be made safely without overstating outcomes.

## Sprint 6: Validation and Council Re-Review
Goal: Confirm A+ readiness under the same council rubric.

Deliverables:
- Re-run outplacement buyer council and Voss/Cialdini/Horstman council.
- Scorecard of old vs new grades by criterion.
- Residual risk backlog with owners and target dates.

Success criteria:
- Blended outplacement buyer score >= 9.0/10.
- Voss, Cialdini, and Horstman grades each at A or above with A+ plan in backlog.

## Acceptance Matrix
- Commercial clarity: A+
- Differentiation: A+
- Governance: A+
- Operator assets: A+
- Counselor adoption: A+
- Trust/procurement: A+

## Implementation Notes
- Use anonymized peer evidence until partner permissions allow named references.
- Keep claim discipline strict: observed cohort outcomes first, directional pilot indicators second.
- Treat pilot as a decision mechanism; avoid commitment pressure language.
