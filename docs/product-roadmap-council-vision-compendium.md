## Cross-Council Consensus

1. Trust and reliability are direct growth levers.
2. Activation and retention loops must be designed intentionally.
3. Partner-led B2B channels should be systemized, not ad hoc.
4. Decision quality should be probabilistic and believability-weighted.
5. Moat-building features should outrank low-leverage expansion.
