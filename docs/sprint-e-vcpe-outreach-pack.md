## Positioning

Use this framing in every message:
- Trigger: reference a visible portfolio leadership transition or transformation event.
- Problem: fund needs a fast, practical transition support option.
- Offer: 10-minute walkthrough; setup in 20 minutes; lower cost than traditional outplacement.
