# Fractional BD Hiring Scorecard and Interview Script

Use this to evaluate fractional Business Development candidates for a high bar, founder-lean startup motion.

## 1) Non-Negotiables (Auto-Reject If Missing)

- No recent hands-on outbound ownership in the last 12-18 months.
- Cannot show a credible path from cold outbound to qualified meetings in under 90 days.
- Cannot explain ICP narrowing with examples.
- Cannot provide concrete weekly metrics from prior engagements.
- Relies on opens/clicks only without pipeline or meeting quality outcomes.

## 2) Weighted Scorecard (100 Points)

Pass threshold for a high bar: **85+ overall**, with no category under **7/10** in sections A-D.

### A. Outcome Proof (30 points)

- Built qualified pipeline from low baseline (0-10)
- Time-to-first-qualified-meeting (0-10)
- Time-to-repeatable weekly output (0-10)

Scoring guide:
- 9-10: Specific outcomes, dates, conversion data, and context.
- 7-8: Strong outcomes but limited instrumentation detail.
- 5-6: Some wins, attribution unclear.
- <5: Vague claims.

### B. ICP and Messaging Rigor (20 points)

- ICP segmentation quality and prioritization logic (0-10)
- Message testing discipline (hypotheses, iterations, winners) (0-10)

### C. Execution System and Operating Cadence (20 points)

- Weekly operating rhythm and accountability structure (0-10)
- Process quality: list building, personalization, QA, follow-up loops (0-10)

### D. Signal Quality and Qualification Judgment (15 points)

- Ability to distinguish curiosity vs buying intent (0-5)
- Quality standards for qualifying meetings (0-5)
- Handoff quality to founder/sales call owner (0-5)

### E. Startup Fit and Founder Leverage (15 points)

- Ability to execute with limited support and moving priorities (0-5)
- Communication quality and speed of iteration (0-5)
- Ownership mindset and decision-making autonomy (0-5)

## 3) Interview Script (45-60 Minutes)

## Section 1: Proof of Outcomes (10 minutes)

Ask:
- "Walk me through one engagement where you started from little/no pipeline and produced qualified meetings in under 90 days."
- "What were your exact weekly numbers in weeks 1-4 and weeks 5-8?"
- "Which metrics improved first, and what did you change to cause that?"

Listen for:
- Baseline -> interventions -> measured change.
- Numbers tied to dates and channel mix.
- Clear causality, not generic storytelling.

## Section 2: ICP and Positioning Discipline (10 minutes)

Ask:
- "How did you narrow ICP from broad TAM to first two target segments?"
- "Show how your message changed between segment A and segment B."
- "What is your kill criteria for a weak segment?"

Listen for:
- Segment logic based on pain urgency, access, and conversion probability.
- Concrete message variants and why they worked.

## Section 3: Execution Mechanics (10 minutes)

Ask:
- "What is your weekly outbound operating cadence from list build through follow-up?"
- "How do you QA list quality and personalization quality before send?"
- "How do you prevent channel drift and random activity?"

Listen for:
- Repeatable weekly system.
- Quality gates before volume.
- Explicit stop/go thresholds.

## Section 4: Qualification and Handoff Quality (10 minutes)

Ask:
- "Define a qualified meeting in your own words."
- "Give an example of a meeting you rejected as unqualified and why."
- "What context do you hand off so founder time is not wasted?"

Listen for:
- Strong disqualification discipline.
- Structured meeting notes and pre-call context standards.

## Section 5: Startup Fit and Constraints (5-10 minutes)

Ask:
- "What do you need from me each week for this to succeed?"
- "Where have founder-led processes slowed you down before?"
- "When do you escalate vs decide independently?"

Listen for:
- Low-dependency operator behavior.
- Clear assumptions and escalation boundaries.

## 4) Live Case Exercise (Required)

Send candidate a short brief 24 hours before final round:

- Product: Starting Monday
- Audience: one chosen segment (example: executive coaches)
- Goal: booked qualified intro calls
- Constraint: no paid ads, 30-day runway

Ask for a 1-page plan and 10-minute walkthrough:
- ICP micro-segments (top 2)
- 2 outbound hypotheses
- 2 message variants per segment
- 4-week activity + KPI plan
- Qualification criteria and handoff template

Score case on:
- Clarity (0-5)
- Specificity (0-5)
- Practicality (0-5)
- Metric discipline (0-5)
- Message quality (0-5)

High bar: **22/25+**.

## 5) Reference Check Script (15 minutes)

Ask former client/manager:
- "What were they explicitly hired to change?"
- "What measurable outcomes did they produce and in what timeframe?"
- "How strong was their weekly communication and accountability?"
- "What broke under pressure?"
- "Would you rehire them for a zero-to-pipeline assignment?"

Red flags:
- Praise without measurable outcomes.
- Heavy strategy, weak execution.
- Required excessive founder support.

## 6) Pilot Structure (Recommended Before Full Commitment)

Duration: 30 days

Required deliverables by week:
- Week 1: ICP selection, messaging framework, list quality rubric, KPI baseline
- Week 2: Sequence live, QA checks live, first iteration report
- Week 3: Conversion analysis and message/segment iteration
- Week 4: Repeatable weekly cadence and recommendation memo

Core pilot metrics:
- Positive reply rate
- Qualified meeting rate
- Meeting show rate
- Qualified-to-next-step rate
- Founder time per qualified meeting

Go decision:
- Continue only if quality and conversion trend in the right direction by week 4 with clear causal learnings.

## 7) Decision Framework

- **Hire now**: 85+ scorecard, 22/25+ case, strong references, clear startup fit.
- **Conditional hire**: 78-84 with one fixable gap and strong pilot outcomes.
- **Do not hire**: <78, weak qualification discipline, vague outcomes, or poor references.
