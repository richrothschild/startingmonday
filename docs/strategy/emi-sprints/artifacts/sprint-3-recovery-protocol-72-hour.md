# Sprint 3 Artifact: 72-Hour Recovery Protocol

Date: 2026-05-25
Owner: Product and Coaching Advisor
Status: Completed

## Objective

Provide an immediate, low-friction protocol for displaced executives to re-establish control and momentum in the first 72 hours.

## Protocol Design

Day 1: Stabilize

1. Capture transition context and constraints.
2. Define one clear operating objective for the week.
3. Complete two low-effort momentum actions.

Day 2: Rebuild

1. Create top-10 relationship list.
2. Prioritize top-5 target organizations.
3. Send first wave outreach to two contacts.

Day 3: Activate

1. Finalize seven-day action plan.
2. Set daily cadence reminders.
3. Complete first weekly review checkpoint.

## Completion Definition

1. Day 1 complete with all stabilization actions done.
2. Day 2 complete with relationship and target actions done.
3. Day 3 complete with cadence plan active.

## KPI Link

Primary KPI: day-3 protocol completion rate.
