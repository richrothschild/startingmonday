# Proof Asset 03: Coach-Linked Momentum Uplift

Date: 2026-05-25
Owner: Content and Partnerships
Status: Published

## Claim

Participants in coach-linked workflows show stronger weekly completion than non-coach control cohorts.

## Evidence Block

1. Denominator: 126 coach-linked participants and 174 non-coach controls
2. Timeframe: trailing 30 days ending 2026-05-25
3. Metric: weekly action completion rate
4. Confidence: Directional

## Practical Interpretation

Coach-linked accountability appears to improve behavior consistency and may support stronger partner outcomes.
