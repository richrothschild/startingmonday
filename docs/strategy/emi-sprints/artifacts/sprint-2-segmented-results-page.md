# Sprint 2 Artifact: Segmented Results Page

Date: 2026-05-25
Owner: Product Design
Status: Completed

## Objective

Deliver persona-specific score outputs with immediate next actions that increase onboarding conversion.

## Results Structure

1. Score banner
- Shows EMI score and band.
- Shows one-sentence interpretation.

2. Top two risk factors
- Lists the two highest-impact gaps from assessment answers.

3. Recommended mode
- Displaced mode, Optionality mode, or Board-track mode.

4. Seven-day action plan
- Three daily actions with low-friction completion design.

5. Onboarding CTA
- Primary CTA: Start my operating plan.
- Secondary CTA: Send me my plan summary.

## Copy Rules

1. Start with outcome language, then method.
2. Avoid generic motivational language.
3. Keep all recommendations action-ready in one session.

## Persona Recommendations

1. Displaced
- Start 72-hour recovery protocol.
- Prioritize momentum re-establishment.

2. Optionality
- Start weekly cadence with low-noise digest.
- Prioritize relationship maintenance and role clarity.

3. Board-track
- Start long-cycle governance plan.
- Prioritize narrative quality and long-horizon signals.
