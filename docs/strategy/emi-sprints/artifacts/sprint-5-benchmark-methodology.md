# Sprint 5 Artifact: Benchmark Methodology

Date: 2026-05-25
Owner: Data and Legal
Status: Completed

## Objective

Establish an evidence standard so all tier-1 EMI claims are denominator-backed and confidence-labeled.

## Methodology Standard

Every benchmark claim must include:

1. Metric definition
2. Denominator
3. Timeframe
4. Confidence label

## Data Source Rules

1. Use only de-identified aggregate product data.
2. Exclude cohorts below minimum sample size threshold.
3. Log extraction date and query owner.

## Confidence Labels

1. High confidence: stable sample and low variance.
2. Medium confidence: moderate sample or moderate variance.
3. Directional: early signal with limited sample.

## Publication Guardrails

1. No tier-1 claim may be published without denominator.
2. Claims must map to a documented metric definition.
3. Claims must include timeframe in readable language.
