# Sprint 2 Artifact: EMI Assessment Flow

Date: 2026-05-25
Owner: Product
Status: Completed

## Objective

Define the assessment flow, persona-aware scoring model, and completion logic for EMI Assessment v1.

## Flow Steps

1. Entry screen
- Captures persona and transition context.
- Sets expectation: 7-10 minutes to complete.

2. Baseline block
- Captures current operating rhythm, follow-through consistency, and transition pressure.

3. Momentum block
- Captures weekly cadence strength, relationship discipline, and action completion consistency.

4. Readiness block
- Captures role-target clarity, narrative clarity, and opportunity timing readiness.

5. Friction block
- Captures burnout, cognitive load, and recovery risk.

6. Review and submit
- Confirms responses and computes total score.

## Persona Weighting Model

1. Displaced executive
- Momentum 35 percent
- Recovery readiness 30 percent
- Role clarity 20 percent
- Cognitive load 15 percent

2. In-role optionality executive
- Relationship cadence 30 percent
- Opportunity awareness 30 percent
- Narrative readiness 25 percent
- Weekly discipline 15 percent

3. Board-track executive
- Governance readiness 30 percent
- Long-cycle cadence 30 percent
- Narrative depth 25 percent
- Follow-through 15 percent

## Scoring Bands

1. 0-39: Reactive risk
2. 40-64: Unstable momentum
3. 65-79: Controlled momentum
4. 80-100: Execution-ready momentum

## Completion Definition

Assessment is complete when:

1. all required fields are answered
2. persona weighting is applied
3. score and recommendation payload is generated
