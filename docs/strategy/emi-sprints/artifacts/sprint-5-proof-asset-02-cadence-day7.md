# Proof Asset 02: Cadence Adherence and Day-7 Return

Date: 2026-05-25
Owner: Content and Data
Status: Published

## Claim

Users with at least three completed actions in their first week show higher day-7 return than low-adherence users.

## Evidence Block

1. Denominator: 389 activated users across segments
2. Timeframe: trailing 60 days ending 2026-05-25
3. Metric: first-week action adherence and day-7 return
4. Confidence: High

## Practical Interpretation

Low-friction completion in week one predicts short-horizon retention better than passive usage.
