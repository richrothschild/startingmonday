# Sprint 1 Artifact: Trust Center Starter Pack

Date: 2026-05-25
Owner: Legal and Ops
Status: Draft for Sprint 1 execution

## Purpose

Define the minimum trust baseline required for partner, procurement, and legal review during EMI rollout.

## Data Boundary Model

1. Customer-provided profile and workflow data remains customer-account scoped.
2. Partner views are role-limited and cannot access cross-account data.
3. Counselor and coach views expose progress state, not unnecessary personal detail.
4. Benchmark outputs are aggregate and de-identified.

## Data Use Policy

1. Product operations: service delivery and reliability.
2. Benchmark use: aggregate trend analysis only.
3. No claims are published from single-account anecdotes.
4. Sensitive fields are minimized in analytics exports.

## Claims Policy

All major external claims must include:

1. denominator
2. timeframe
3. confidence label
4. metric definition

Claims that do not include all four are not publishable.

## KPI Definition Baseline

1. Assessment completion rate = completed assessments divided by started assessments.
2. Day-7 return rate = activated users with one or more return sessions by day 7.
3. B2B pilot conversion = approved pilots divided by qualified pilot opportunities.
4. Category adoption rate = templates using approved EMI language divided by audited templates.

## Review and Approval Flow

1. Draft owner prepares artifact.
2. Legal and ops review controls and definitions.
3. Founder office validates public-language alignment.
4. Publish to partner and procurement package.

## Required Artifacts for External Review

1. Positioning brief
2. Objection library
3. Claims checklist
4. KPI dictionary excerpt
5. Pilot runbook excerpt

## Open Items for Sprint 1 Close

1. Final legal owner signoff name
2. Final approved claims checklist version
3. Procurement-facing PDF export
