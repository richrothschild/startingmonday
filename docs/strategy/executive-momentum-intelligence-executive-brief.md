# Executive Brief: EMI 90-Day Execution

Date: 2026-05-25
Owner: Founder Office
Status: Ready to Execute

## Decision

We are standardizing category and product execution under one umbrella: Executive Momentum Intelligence (EMI).

## Why Now

1. Current market language is strong but inconsistent.
2. Buyer trust requires denominator-backed proof, not narrative alone.
3. Retention depends on daily behavior loops, not one-time insight delivery.

## What We Are Executing

1. Objection system: member-by-member objections converted into owned deliverables.
2. KPI discipline: one primary KPI per objection.
3. Sprint execution: six two-week sprints with mandatory exit gates.

## 90-Day Outcome Targets

1. EMI language in at least 85 percent of top-funnel assets.
2. EMI assessment completion at or above 40 percent.
3. Daily loop day-7 return at or above 55 percent.
4. Three denominator-backed benchmark proof artifacts published.
5. B2B pilot conversion from qualified opportunities at or above 25 percent.

## Sprint Sequence

1. Sprint 1: category lock, objection library, trust baseline
2. Sprint 2: EMI assessment v1 launch
3. Sprint 3: daily momentum loop v1
4. Sprint 4: coach and outplacement enablement
5. Sprint 5: benchmark and proof system
6. Sprint 6: tuning, reliability lock, and capstone

## Governance

1. Weekly scorecard review every Friday.
2. No sprint closes without evidence linked to exit gate.
3. Any unmet gate must have dated remediation and named owner.

## Immediate Ask

Approve Sprint 1 as active and enforce no parallel strategic initiatives that dilute EMI delivery in this 90-day window.
