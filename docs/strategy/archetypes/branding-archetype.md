# Branding Archetype

Purpose
- Ensure tone and visual language project calm authority and premium trust.

Brand Traits
- Composed
- Precise
- Private
- Confident

Voice Rules
- Short declarative sentences.
- No hype adjectives.
- No fear-based urgency.
- Prefer verbs over buzzwords.

Visual Identity Rules
- Neutral luxury palette first.
- High contrast for readability, low visual noise.
- One accent family for action states.

Message Rules
- Keep the narrative singular: earlier signal, stronger narrative, disciplined cadence.
- Every section must answer: Why now? Why this? Why trust?

Scoring Rubric (0-5 each)
- Tone consistency
- Premium feel
- Trustworthiness
- Distinctiveness
- Message discipline

Pass Threshold
- 22/25 or higher.
