# Luxury Brand Archetype

Purpose
- Apply luxury digital design rules similar to premium hospitality/fashion experiences.

Luxury UI Rules
- Restraint over density.
- Premium typography scale and calm rhythm.
- Editorial image framing, never edge-to-edge clutter.
- Subtle surfaces, subtle shadows, soft borders.

Color Rules
- Primary canvas: warm ivory/stone neutrals.
- Text: deep charcoal.
- Accent: muted gold/champagne only for emphasis.
- Avoid neon or saturated marketing hues.

Composition Rules
- Strong negative space around hero and media.
- Asymmetric balance where useful, but aligned baselines.
- One focal visual at a time.

Micro-Polish Rules
- Border radii consistent.
- Shadow depth low and controlled.
- Button states refined, not flashy.

Scoring Rubric (0-5 each)
- Visual restraint
- Editorial composition
- Material quality feel
- Color discipline
- Premium coherence

Pass Threshold
- 23/25 or higher.
