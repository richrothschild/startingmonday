# Product Archetype

Purpose
- Define what the product must communicate in one screen for immediate executive relevance.

Audience
- Executive in active or near-active transition.

Core Promise
- Find the right role earlier, control your story, and run a calm weekly cadence.

Non-Negotiables
- Show concrete workflow, not generic inspiration.
- Show privacy posture clearly.
- Show immediate first action in under 10 seconds.

Content Rules
- One primary headline, one supporting sentence.
- Three capability pillars max.
- One primary CTA, one secondary CTA.
- No feature sprawl.

Visual Rules
- Evidence-first hierarchy.
- Generous whitespace around decision areas.
- Minimal decorative motion.

Scoring Rubric (0-5 each)
- Clarity of promise
- Relevance to executive decisions
- Time-to-understand
- Actionability of next step
- Trust signal quality

Pass Threshold
- 21/25 or higher.
