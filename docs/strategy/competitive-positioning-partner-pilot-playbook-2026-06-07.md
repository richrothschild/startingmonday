# Partner Pilot Playbook: Coaches and Outplacement

Date: 2026-06-07
Owner: Partnerships + Product + Growth
Duration: 90-day pilot motion

## Purpose
Launch and validate two channel pilots that leverage Starting Monday's differentiation:
- Coach channel pilot
- Outplacement partner pilot

Goal:
- Create repeatable partner acquisition and expansion motion with measurable ROI.

## Pilot Design Principles
1. Start narrow with high-fit partners.
2. Co-design workflow with pilot partners, not in isolation.
3. Instrument outcomes from day one.
4. Tie every partner conversation to explicit ROI narrative.

## Channel 1: Executive Coach Pilot

### ICP
- Independent or boutique executive coaches
- Focus on transition-ready senior tech leaders
- Managing 5-30 active clients
- Existing pain around visibility between sessions

### Offer
- Shared client campaign visibility
- Signal-driven target company intelligence
- Narrative prep briefs for coaching sessions
- Monthly outcome report for coach and client

### Pilot Packaging
- Duration: 8-12 weeks
- Cohort size: 1-5 coaches
- Starter package:
  - coach onboarding session
  - shared workflow setup
  - weekly digest report
  - monthly KPI review

### Activation Checklist
1. Confirm partner goals and current workflow.
2. Define baseline metrics per coach cohort.
3. Configure shared view and role permissions.
4. Run first client pipeline import.
5. Schedule weekly check-ins.

### Success Metrics
- Coach engagement rate (weekly active usage)
- Client activation rate
- Narrative open and outreach action rates
- Coach-reported session effectiveness improvement
- Conversion to paid partner plan

## Channel 2: Outplacement Pilot

### ICP
- Outplacement firms with executive transition programs
- 20-200 active program participants
- Need governance-ready reporting for sponsors

### Offer
- Cohort command view for program operators
- Signal and prep infrastructure for counselors and candidates
- Governance-friendly reporting artifacts for sponsor check-ins
- Standardized operating cadence templates

### Pilot Packaging
- Duration: 8-12 weeks
- Cohort size: 1-3 firms
- Starter package:
  - operator onboarding
  - counselor enablement workshop
  - sponsor metric dictionary
  - weekly operating report and monthly executive readout

### Activation Checklist
1. Confirm sponsor success criteria.
2. Capture baseline cohort outcomes.
3. Configure cohort dashboards.
4. Train counselors on signal + prep + action workflow.
5. Issue first weekly partner report.

### Success Metrics
- Candidate engagement and completion metrics
- Outreach action progression
- Program-level conversation conversion
- Sponsor confidence score
- Expansion or renewal intent

---

## Shared Pilot Motion

### Stage 1: Prospecting (Weeks 1-2)
- Build target list (10 coaches, 5 outplacement firms)
- Send role-specific outreach sequence
- Book discovery calls

### Stage 2: Discovery and Qualification (Weeks 2-4)
- Assess problem intensity and workflow fit
- Confirm willingness to run pilot with measurable outcomes
- Disqualify low-fit partners early

### Stage 3: Pilot Launch (Weeks 4-6)
- Finalize pilot scope and baseline metrics
- Complete onboarding and implementation
- Begin weekly reporting

### Stage 4: Midpoint Optimization (Weeks 7-9)
- Review KPI movement and friction points
- Tune workflow and communication cadence
- Validate partner value perception

### Stage 5: Conversion and Expansion (Weeks 10-12)
- Present outcome summary
- Run renewal/expansion discussion
- Capture testimonials and case-study permissions

## Partner ROI Narrative Templates

### Coach ROI Narrative
"Starting Monday increases coaching leverage by replacing status-gathering with live campaign visibility and role-specific prep context, so sessions focus on strategic decision quality instead of operational catch-up."

### Outplacement ROI Narrative
"Starting Monday gives operators and counselors one measurable execution system, improving cohort consistency and sponsor confidence with structured weekly evidence instead of fragmented updates."

## Weekly Pilot Cadence
- Monday: Partner KPI extraction and internal review
- Tuesday: Coach/outplacement office hours
- Wednesday: Workflow optimization changes
- Thursday: Partner update notes and next actions
- Friday: Leadership pilot scoreboard and risk review

## Pilot Scorecard (Required)
1. Partner health
- active users
- weekly usage depth
- feature adoption

2. Candidate/coachee outcomes
- signal run rate
- narrative open rate
- outreach start rate
- company add rate

3. Business outcomes
- conversion to paid pilot
- expansion potential
- testimonial/case-study readiness

## Risks and Mitigations
Risk 1: Partner wants full custom solution too early
- Mitigation: enforce pilot scope with clear template boundaries

Risk 2: Low adoption after onboarding
- Mitigation: mandatory week-1 workflow checkpoint and admin nudges

Risk 3: ROI unclear to sponsor
- Mitigation: baseline capture + monthly delta reporting using agreed metric dictionary

## Exit Criteria
Pilot motion is validated when:
1. Minimum 3 coach pilots and 2 outplacement pilots complete with measurable outcomes.
2. At least 2 partners convert to paid continuation.
3. At least 2 publishable partner case studies are approved.
4. A repeatable pilot-to-renewal playbook is documented and accepted by GTM leadership.
