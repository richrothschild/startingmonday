# Leadership Brief: Strengthening Starting Monday Position

Date: 2026-06-07
Audience: Leadership team
Length: One-page operating brief

## Decision Context
Starting Monday has a defensible product advantage (signal intelligence + prep + campaign execution), but adjacent market leaders outperform us on trust signaling, packaging clarity, and distribution channels.

Core leadership decision:
- Stay focused on category ownership instead of broad feature expansion.

Category to own:
- Signal-guided executive campaign operating system.

## Strategic Thesis
If we make our differentiation obvious, measurable, and repeatable, we can out-convert larger competitors in our specific segment (self-directed senior tech leaders) even without matching their brand scale.

## What We Will Do in 90 Days
1. Clarify buyer narrative
- Tight message hierarchy on who we serve, why we win, and how we differ from job boards, coaching, and done-for-you services.

2. Publish hard proof
- Launch public proof center and role-specific case studies to reduce buyer skepticism.

3. Reinforce moat in product
- Surface signal freshness, prep quality, and campaign health as visible value and conversion drivers.

4. Launch partner channels
- Activate coach and outplacement pilots with partner reporting and renewal packet.

## Expected Business Impact
By Day 90 we target:
- +20% homepage-to-signup conversion
- +20% trial-to-paid conversion
- +20% core workflow WAU
- +10% 30-day retention
- 20-30% qualified pipeline from partner channels

## Non-Negotiable Focus Rules
1. No drift into generic job board functionality.
2. No shift to service-heavy done-for-you operating model.
3. No major roadmap additions that do not directly improve:
- buyer clarity
- proof/trust
- moat visibility
- partner distribution

## Investment Priorities
Priority 1: Positioning and conversion surfaces
- Homepage, role pages, comparison pages, pricing clarity

Priority 2: Proof and trust assets
- Public KPI cards, source/method notes, role-specific outcomes

Priority 3: Product signal/prep execution score system
- Signal freshness, prep quality, campaign health

Priority 4: Channel pilots
- Coach and outplacement pilot bundles + reporting

## Risks and Leadership Mitigation
Risk 1: We have product strength but weak market belief
- Leadership action: mandate proof publication cadence and evidence quality standard

Risk 2: Team fragmentation across too many initiatives
- Leadership action: enforce 90-day constraint and deprioritize non-core work

Risk 3: Partner pilots fail due to weak economics framing
- Leadership action: approve clear ROI narrative and pilot success definition before outreach

## Operating Cadence
Weekly:
- KPI review
- Experiment decisions
- Cross-functional unblock

Monthly:
- Gate review (Day 30 / Day 60 / Day 90 milestones)
- Resource rebalance based on measured results

## Ask to Leadership
Approve the 90-day plan and lock the scope to this thesis:
- We win by proving and amplifying our signal-prep-execution advantage, not by becoming a generic alternative to coaching or job boards.
