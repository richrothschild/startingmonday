# Quick-Start Guide: Executive Coaches

**Audience:** Independent coaches, boutique firm coaches, and enterprise-sponsored coach cohorts
**Time to first value:** Under 20 minutes (preview), 30 days (full pilot)
**URL:** startingmonday.app/for-coaches

---

## What Starting Monday does for you

Starting Monday gives you a private operating layer so clients arrive prepared, sessions stay strategic, and your coaching authority stays intact. You see signals, prep quality, and momentum - your client controls what you see.

---

## Step 1 - Run the coach preview (10 min)

1. Go to **startingmonday.app/for-coaches**
2. Click **Request the coach preview**
3. You will see: the client-side workflow, your visibility settings, the trust boundary, and proof from the Jan-May 2026 pilot cohort
4. Decide whether it fits your practice model before committing to anything

> No client data is required for the preview. This is a review-first motion by design.

---

## Step 2 - Choose your partner path

| Coach model | Best entry point |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Independent coach, 1-15 clients | Solo plan, refer clients via partner link |
| Boutique firm, 15-75 clients | Boutique plan, shared cohort dashboard |
| Enterprise-sponsored program | Outplacement plan, governance pack included |
| Search-adjacent coach | See /for-coaches/search-affiliate |

Apply at **startingmonday.app/partners** (free enrollment, 20% recurring commission on referrals).

---

## Step 3 - Set up your first client (5 min)

1. Log into the coach dashboard at **Dashboard > Coach > Clients**
2. Send your client an invitation link
3. Your client completes their own setup (target list, mode, preferences)
4. You receive a notification when they are active

---

## Step 4 - Use the pre-session snapshot

Before each client session:
1. Go to **Dashboard > Coach > Clients > [Client name]**
2. Open **Session Snapshot**
3. You see: what changed since last session, current pipeline status, momentum markers, confidence level, and one recommended talking point

This replaces the first 15-20 minutes of most sessions that are typically spent rebuilding context.

---

## Step 5 - Review the weekly activity summary

Each week:
1. Go to **Dashboard > Coach > Portfolio**
2. Review: active vs. drifting clients, stalled pipeline alerts, prep quality scores, confidence trend
3. Identify clients who need intervention before problems compound

---

## 30-day pilot scorecard

Run 2-3 clients through the platform for 30 days, then measure:

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Session context-rebuild time | Down from 20 min to under 10 min |
| Client preparation quality | Client arrives with pipeline status ready |
| Between-session momentum | Client takes at least 1 action per week |
| Client confidence trend | Stable or improving at week 4 |

Decide from evidence, not theory.

---

## Trust boundary (important)

- **Clients control your access.** They can grant read-only view of signals and pipeline, or full prep brief access.
- **No recruiter visibility.** Nothing crosses to recruiters without explicit client permission.
- **Your coaching authority is intact.** The platform handles execution; you own strategy.
