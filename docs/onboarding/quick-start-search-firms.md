# Quick-Start Guide: Executive Search Firms

**Audience:** Partners, delivery leads, and candidate success owners at retained executive search firms
**Time to first value:** First candidate prep brief, within 1 hour
**URL:** startingmonday.app/search-firms

---

## What Starting Monday does for your firm

Starting Monday strengthens kickoff quality, shortlist velocity, and candidate readiness on retained mandates. Candidates arrive prepared at first-round interviews. Search professionals reduce prep admin and deliver a demonstrably better candidate experience.

---

## Step 1 - Review the firm-level value case (15 min)

1. Go to **startingmonday.app/search-firms**
2. Review how Starting Monday maps to your three key delivery stages:
   - **Kickoff:** mandate framing, candidate brief preparation, role-specific context
   - **Shortlist:** candidate readiness scores, preparation quality benchmarking
   - **Placement:** interview-arc tracking, post-offer follow-through
3. See a sample brief at **startingmonday.app/search-firms/sample-cfo-brief**

| Role | Entry point |

---

## Step 3 - Run your first candidate brief (30-45 min)

For any active retained search:
1. Request a firm account at **startingmonday.app/search-firms**
2. Add the mandate: target role, company, and stage of search
3. Add the candidate: seniority, background, key transitions
4. The platform generates:
   - Role-specific interview prep brief
   - Likely board or hiring committee questions
   - Mandate context and company background
   - Suggested talking points and positioning

---

## Step 4 - Set up the candidate

If you want the candidate to use the platform directly:
1. Send the candidate an invitation from the mandate dashboard
2. The candidate sets up their own account (free for candidates)
3. They access their prep brief, signals, and interview tracker
4. You can see their prep activity and confidence markers before each round

---

## Step 5 - Use the kickoff quality checklist

Before the first client kickoff call for any new mandate:
1. Go to **Dashboard > Mandates > [Mandate name] > Kickoff prep**
2. Use the structured checklist: position framing, success criteria, committee dynamics, search timeline
3. The platform surfaces relevant market intelligence and comparable role benchmarks

## Trust and confidentiality

- Candidate data is visible only to the search firm and the candidate.
- No data crosses to competing firms or employers without explicit authorization.
- All mandate data is isolated per firm account.
