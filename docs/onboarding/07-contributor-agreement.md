## 1. Scope of Engagement

Contributor agrees to provide software development services to Starting Monday on a pro bono (unpaid) basis, effective the date above. The initial scope is feature development and quality improvement work on the Starting Monday web application and worker service. Specific work items are agreed upon at the weekly Tuesday sync and via async communication.

This is not an employment relationship. Contributor is an independent volunteer contributor with no obligation to work any specific number of hours per week.

Either party may end this engagement at any time with no notice required.

---

## 2. Confidentiality

Contributor agrees to keep all of the following confidential during and after the engagement:

- Source code, architecture, and technical design of Starting Monday
- User data, usage patterns, and product analytics
- Business strategy, roadmap, financial information, and pricing
- Customer and partner relationships, names, and communications
- Any information shared in the course of working on the project that is not publicly available

Contributor will not share, publish, or disclose any of the above to any third party without prior written approval from the Founder.

Publicly available information (code in a public repo, published blog posts, public pricing) is not covered by this confidentiality obligation.

This confidentiality obligation survives the end of the engagement indefinitely.

---

## 3. Intellectual Property

All work product created by Contributor in the course of this engagement — including code, documentation, design, and any derivative works — is the sole and exclusive property of Richard Rothschild and Starting Monday from the moment of creation.

Contributor assigns to the Founder all right, title, and interest in any such work product, including all intellectual property rights, without further compensation.

Contributor agrees to execute any documents the Founder reasonably requests to confirm or perfect this assignment.

If Contributor uses any pre-existing tools, libraries, or frameworks in their work, they agree to use only those that are compatible with commercial use (MIT, Apache 2.0, or similar permissive licenses). Contributor will flag any GPL or copyleft dependency before including it in the codebase.

---

## 4. No Competing Activity

During the engagement, Contributor will not use knowledge gained through this engagement to build a directly competing product targeting the same user base (senior technology executives in active job search).

---

## 5. Data Access

Contributor will have access to the production Supabase database for development purposes. This access includes real user data.

Contributor agrees to:
- Access user data only as required to complete development work
- Not export, copy, or store user data outside the production system
- Report any accidental data exposure to the Founder immediately
- Never use user data for any purpose other than platform development

This obligation survives the end of the engagement.

---

## 6. Access Return

Upon end of engagement, Contributor agrees to:
- Return or destroy any copies of `.env.local`, API keys, or credentials shared during the engagement
- Not retain access to any systems (GitHub, Railway, Supabase, Resend, etc.) — the Founder will revoke access

---

## 7. Future Equity / Compensation

This engagement is currently unpaid. Nothing in this agreement creates any right to equity, compensation, revenue sharing, or any other financial interest in Starting Monday.

Any future compensation arrangement — including but not limited to equity, advisory shares, or payment — will be documented in a separate written agreement signed by both parties. No oral commitment creates any right to future compensation.
