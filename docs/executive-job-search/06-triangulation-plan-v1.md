# Executive Job Search Triangulation Plan v1

Updated: 2026-05-31

## Objective

Build a behavior model for executive job search without current product users by triangulating:

- synthetic council hypotheses
- verified public sources
- expert interviews
- early pilot behavior evidence

## 4-Week Plan

### Week 1: Synthetic Council Expansion

Outputs:
- expanded persona set
- role x stage hypothesis matrix
- top derailer list per segment

Questions to answer:
- what does each role optimize for?
- what is the dominant fear in each transition type?
- which behaviors predict momentum loss?

### Week 2: Source Extraction

Use only verified sources to validate or challenge hypotheses.

Sources to prioritize:
- BLS role summaries
- O*NET work activity structure
- LinkedIn Economic Graph opportunity / signaling pages
- Spencer Stuart transition and decision frameworks

Questions to answer:
- what role differences are structural vs anecdotal?
- which decision criteria appear repeatedly?
- where do public expert frameworks converge?

### Week 3: Expert Interviews

Interview 12–20 people across:
- executive coaches
- search consultants
- recruiters
- outplacement specialists
- board advisors

Questions to answer:
- what actually derails executives in search?
- which behaviors differ by role and seniority?
- what interventions get used in the wild?

### Week 4: Prototype and Measure

Build the first internal product prototype around:
- candidate segmentation
- behavior diagnosis
- intervention recommendation
- evidence tagging

Measurement:
- expert agreement with behavior labels
- quality of recommended intervention
- perceived relevance by role segment

## Confidence Tiers

- Tier A: source-backed and expert-convergent
- Tier B: expert-backed or source-backed but not both
- Tier C: synthetic hypothesis only

## Minimum Product Rule

Only Tier A/B should drive default product behavior.
Tier C can be used in experiments, prompts, and internal tools.

## Deliverables for the First Pilot

- behavior ontology v1
- role-stage matrix v1
- derailer taxonomy v1
- intervention library v1
- evidence register v1
