# Candidate Scoring Rubric v1

Updated: 2026-05-31

## Purpose

Score executive candidates on search readiness and transition health, not just résumé quality.

## Scoring Dimensions

Each dimension is scored from 1 to 5.

### 1. Segment Fit
How well the candidate’s story matches the target role segment.

1 = unclear or mismatched
3 = plausible but generic
5 = highly specific and role-aligned

### 2. Narrative Clarity
How clearly the candidate explains who they are, what they solved, and why they matter now.

1 = noisy or inconsistent
3 = understandable but broad
5 = concise, credible, and differentiated

### 3. Opportunity Discipline
How well the candidate selects targets and avoids title-chasing.

1 = indiscriminate or title-led
3 = mixed discipline
5 = strong company / challenge / context filter

### 4. Outreach Activation
How effectively the candidate uses network and sponsor channels.

1 = almost no warm activation
3 = some outreach but inconsistent
5 = strong, disciplined network activation

### 5. Process Navigation
How well the candidate reads stakeholders and adapts to the decision system.

1 = confused or passive
3 = partly effective
5 = maps stakeholders and progresses intentionally

### 6. Decision Quality
How well the candidate handles offers and trade-offs.

1 = stuck or reactive
3 = evaluative but inconsistent
5 = explicit, rational, and context-aware

### 7. Resilience
How well the candidate recovers from rejection and uncertainty.

1 = derails after setbacks
3 = mixed stability
5 = recovers quickly and maintains momentum

### 8. Transition Readiness
How ready the candidate is to succeed in the new role if hired.

1 = no first-90-day thinking
3 = some preparation
5 = clear early-win and stakeholder plan

## Score Bands

- 36 to 40: exceptional readiness
- 30 to 35: strong readiness
- 24 to 29: moderate readiness with fixable gaps
- 18 to 23: fragile readiness
- below 18: high-risk transition

## Rubric Weights

Suggested weighted score:

- Segment Fit: 15%
- Narrative Clarity: 15%
- Opportunity Discipline: 10%
- Outreach Activation: 10%
- Process Navigation: 15%
- Decision Quality: 15%
- Resilience: 10%
- Transition Readiness: 10%

## Output Interpretation

### High score, low resilience
Candidate is strong on paper but may need support after rejection or ambiguity.

### High network activation, weak narrative
Candidate is active but not yet differentiated.

### High segment fit, weak decision quality
Candidate may be market-ready but not offer-ready.

### Low context awareness
Candidate may accept the wrong role even if the search looks successful.

## Recommended Use

Use the rubric to drive:
- candidate fit diagnosis
- intervention selection
- coaching focus
- progress tracking across weeks

## Notes

This rubric is intended for executive search behaviors, not general job-seeker scoring.
