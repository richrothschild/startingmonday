# Validated Constructs Catalog

Updated: 2026-05-31
Item covered: 7

## Constructs

1. Self-regulation.
Definition: ability to sustain disciplined behavior under uncertainty.
Signals: cadence adherence, recovery after setbacks, follow-through delay.

2. Networking behavior.
Definition: quality and consistency of sponsor and contact activation.
Signals: outreach initiation, warm-intro density, response rate.

3. Job-search intensity.
Definition: amount and rhythm of search activity.
Signals: weekly actions, active companies, stage movement.

4. Identity threat.
Definition: perceived mismatch between self-image and market role.
Signals: narrative rewrites, avoidance language, decision hesitation.

5. Career adaptability.
Definition: ability to adjust strategy across changing constraints.
Signals: pivot behavior, role reframing speed, intervention uptake.

## Confidence Rule

1. Default interventions should rely on Tier A and Tier B support.
2. Tier C should only influence experiment design.
