# Leadership One-Page Operating Summary

Date: 2026-05-31  
Owner: Product + Research  
Program: Executive Emotional Transition OS

## What We Are Solving

Executives in transition do not fail only because of low activity. They fail when emotional strain degrades behavior quality at key moments: early activation, rejection recovery, and final decision trade-offs. Our current system is strong on behavior taxonomy, but weaker on emotion-state detection and timing-sensitive interventions.

## Why This Matters Now

1. Existing validated work gives us strong foundations: derailer taxonomy, intervention library, construct map, archetypes, and top-50 behavior hypotheses.
2. Current gap is operational, not conceptual: we need better detection, routing, and closed-loop measurement.
3. This is a near-term leverage point for conversion, decision quality, and onboarding outcomes.

## Strategic Thesis

If we infer operational emotional risk states from behavior signals and route stage-appropriate interventions, executives will sustain higher-quality search momentum, recover faster from setbacks, and choose better-fit opportunities.

## Scope

In scope:
1. Emotion-state schema and scoring.
2. Stage + archetype + confidence-tier intervention routing.
3. Context-aware offer decision support.
4. Recovery and outcomes telemetry.

Out of scope:
1. Clinical diagnosis.
2. Replacing human coaching for high-severity cases.

## 12-Week Delivery Plan

1. Sprints 1-2: detection and routing foundation.
2. Sprints 3-4: recovery loops and decision intelligence.
3. Sprints 5-6: high-stakes board track support, measurement, and rollout readiness.

## Success Metrics

1. 14-day abandonment risk down 25%.
2. Rejection recovery time down 30%.
3. 72-hour follow-up SLA adherence up 20%.
4. Decision lag after final rounds down 20%.
5. Time-to-first-value in onboarding down 15%.

## Leadership Decisions Needed

1. Approve 6-sprint sequence as a single cross-functional epic.
2. Assign dedicated owners for: scoring service, routing engine, decision workspace, and analytics.
3. Confirm pilot cohort and go/no-go threshold for broader rollout in Sprint 6.

## Operating Risks and Mitigations

1. Risk: false positives in emotion-state scoring.  
Mitigation: confidence bands, fallback routing, and human-review QA.

2. Risk: intervention overload in active searches.  
Mitigation: minimal effective intervention rule and weekly load cap.

3. Risk: weak measurement integrity.  
Mitigation: instrument metric definitions before feature launch.

## Next 14 Days

1. Finalize emotion-state schema and signal dictionary.
2. Ship scoring API and routing engine behind feature flags.
3. Stand up pilot dashboard for baseline metric capture.
