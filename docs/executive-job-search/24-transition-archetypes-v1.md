# Transition Archetypes v1

Updated: 2026-05-31
Item covered: 16

## Archetype 1: Forced Exit Recovery

Traits:

1. High urgency.
2. Lower decision quality under time pressure.
3. Increased risk of broad, low-fit outreach.

Recommended interventions:

1. Search rhythm contract.
2. Four-Cs decision worksheet.
3. Rejection recovery protocol.

## Archetype 2: Passive Strategic Upgrade

Traits:

1. Employed and selective.
2. Context and challenge weighted above compensation.
3. Slower but higher-fit movement.

Recommended interventions:

1. Timing and context check.
2. Opportunity discipline review.
3. Board or stakeholder map.

## Archetype 3: Functional Pivot Reframer

Traits:

1. Strong domain experience with role-language mismatch.
2. Narrative rewrite needed before activation.
3. Higher signaling risk in early outreach.

Recommended interventions:

1. Role reframing sprint.
2. Proof-point compression.
3. Segment-specific outreach scripts.

## Archetype 4: Board-Visible Transition

Traits:

1. High reputational sensitivity.
2. Sponsor mapping required.
3. Decision cycles include context and governance signals.

Recommended interventions:

1. Board sponsor map.
2. Timing and context check.
3. First-90-day design.

## Archetype 5: Re-entry after Break

Traits:

1. Elevated identity threat.
2. Lower initial activation confidence.
3. Benefits from small-step motion and rapid wins.

Recommended interventions:

1. Small-step activation plan.
2. Narrative confidence prompts.
3. Weekly cadence reinforcement.
