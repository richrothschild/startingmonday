## Why Co-Author Is the Right Play

Co-authoring with a Forbes Coaches Council member:
- Gets the article in front of 100,000+ coaches and their C-suite clients.
- Gives Rich a Forbes byline without needing to be a coach.
- Gives the coach an original data source, a fresh angle, and a platform partner.
- Delivers social proof and authority (Cialdini) faster than any cold submission.

The pitch to coaches is: "You provide the coaching lens and the Forbes platform. I provide the data, the product insight, and the article research. We co-author a piece that builds your brand and reaches your clients."
