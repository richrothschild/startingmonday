# Synthetic Growth Council Charter

## Mission
Continuously improve landing-page engagement and qualified signup conversion through disciplined weekly experiments.

## Council Seats and Owners
- Product marketing owns messaging
- Design owns UX patterns
- Engineering owns performance and event integrity
- Growth owns experiments and reporting

## Weekly Responsibilities
### Product Marketing
- Own message hierarchy and CTA clarity.
- Approve all headline/subhead tests.
- Ensure above-the-fold copy meets clarity standard.

### Design
- Enforce five-section page architecture maximum.
- Validate mobile readability and visual hierarchy.
- Reduce friction for critical conversion paths.

### Engineering
- Maintain event schema and telemetry reliability.
- Enforce production gate checks before production-bound commits.
- Keep LCP, INP, and CLS within agreed thresholds.

### Growth
- Own backlog scoring, test launch rhythm, and readout quality.
- Publish weekly scorecard and decision log.
- Escalate regressions and coordinate recovery plans.

## Required Weekly Outputs
- Updated scorecard in docs/growth/weekly-metrics.latest.json
- Friday readout in docs/growth/weekly-readout.latest.md
- Updated decisions in docs/growth/weekly-decision-log.md

## Council Decision Standard
- All experiments receive Ship, Iterate, or Kill.
- No unresolved experiment can roll into the next week.
- Guardrail breaches trigger immediate rollback recommendation.
