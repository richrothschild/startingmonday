# Starting Monday — User Success Story Template

Internal use | For capturing user wins for marketing, referrals, and product development

---

## Purpose

Every user who accepts an offer is a living proof point. This template captures the story in a structured format that can be used for anonymous case studies on the website and in partner pitches, social proof in sales conversations, product development insights on what actually moved the needle, and referral prompts to identify who else should know about this.

Collect at offer acceptance. Keep it brief. The goal is a 5-minute conversation, not an interview.

## Example Completed Story (Illustrative)

Situation: A Transformation CIO in search after a restructuring. 18 target companies. Confident in her record but frustrated by the manual overhead of tracking and preparing for conversations.

Challenge: "I was doing the research manually — reading press releases, pulling annual reports, trying to remember what I'd talked about with each contact. It was taking 3–4 hours per call to feel ready."

What she used: Prep brief (primary), daily briefing, company signals, contact tracker.

The moment: "A signal fired on a company I'd been watching — a new CFO hire. I reached out to a contact there the next day. That conversation led directly to a first call with the CEO. The timing would not have happened without the signal."

The outcome: CIO role at a mid-market PE-backed company. Search duration: 5 months.

Quote: "I walked into every conversation with a level of preparation I couldn't have produced on my own in the time I had. That's the edge."
