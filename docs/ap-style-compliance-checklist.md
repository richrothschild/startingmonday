# AP Style Compliance Checklist

Use this checklist to review outreach messages, guides, and public-facing docs for AP style compliance before publishing.

- [ ] Are sentences clear, concise, and active?
- [ ] Is jargon avoided or explained?
- [ ] Are numbers one through nine spelled out, and numerals used for 10 and above?
- [ ] Are dates and times formatted as "May 20, 2026" and "9 a.m."?
- [ ] Is the Oxford comma avoided unless needed for clarity?
- [ ] Are proper nouns and titles capitalized correctly?
- [ ] Are job titles lowercase after a name or when used alone?
- [ ] Are acronyms spelled out on first reference?
- [ ] Is "email" used (not "e-mail")?
- [ ] Are exclamation points avoided?
- [ ] Are em dashes (—) used without spaces for emphasis?
- [ ] Are compound adjectives hyphenated before a noun?
- [ ] Are headlines capitalized only on the first word and proper nouns?
- [ ] Are periods omitted in acronyms (CEO, not C.E.O.)?
- [ ] Is "more than" used with numerals, not "over"?

For more, see docs/ap-style-quick-reference.md or your full AP Stylebook.
