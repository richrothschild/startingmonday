## Backlog Conventions

**Effort sizing:**
- S = Small (< 2 hours)
- M = Medium (2–8 hours, fits in one sprint alongside other work)
- L = Large (8+ hours, may span multiple sprints or require dedicated sprint capacity)

**Status:**
- `[ ]` = Not started
- `[~]` = In progress
- `[x]` = Complete

**Sprint assignment:** Stories marked with a sprint target (e.g., B1, B7) should be pulled into the sprint planning session for that sprint. Stories without a sprint assignment are in the backlog for future planning.

**Relationship to DEV sprints:** Business (BIZ) sprints run in parallel with development (DEV) sprints on the same 2-week cadence. They are planned and reviewed separately. Each sprint planning session covers both tracks. Business stories do not create code; development stories do not create go-to-market assets. Founder owns BIZ track; founder + dev collaborators own DEV track.
