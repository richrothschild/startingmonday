How To Improve Odds of Success (Practical Levers)

1) Enforce kill criteria every Friday
- If DM->call < 5% for 2 weeks, rewrite positioning + list quality before increasing volume.
- If trial->paid < 15% for 2 weeks, pause acquisition spend and focus activation + sales process.
- If week-2 activation < 50%, treat onboarding as the product bottleneck and reprioritize roadmap.

2) Protect founder sales time as a non-negotiable
- Minimum 10 hours/week of direct sales calls and follow-ups.
- Delegate everything else that does not directly improve conversion.

3) Narrow ICP aggressively
- Focus on one wedge at a time (example: VP Product in transition in B2B SaaS).
- Run 2-week messaging sprint per wedge; keep only messages with strong reply rates.

4) Build trust velocity
- Ask for review/testimonial after third successful brief.
- Convert each win into one micro-case snippet (problem, action, result).
- Use snippets across landing pages, outreach, and call decks.

5) Introduce offer urgency without discount addiction
- Keep founding offer capacity-limited and time-boxed.
- Shift urgency messaging from price to outcomes and timing windows.

6) Tighten data discipline
- No campaign runs without UTM tags.
- No experiment considered valid without event integrity checks.
- Single dashboard reviewed weekly with hard decisions.

7) Increase close probability through process
- Every call ends with one explicit next step and date.
- Send recap with payment link same day when there is buying intent.
- Add 7-day check-in meeting at trial start to reduce drift.

8) Build channel optionality early
- Keep direct founder-led sales primary through customer #100.
- Develop coach partner channel in parallel as secondary compounding source.

9) Add a simple board of advisors rhythm
- Monthly 45-minute review with 2-3 trusted operators.
- Bring scorecard, bottlenecks, and one hard decision to force clarity.

10) Pre-commit to downside protection
- Define max monthly spend cap and runway guardrails.
- Trigger immediate cost reductions if two key funnel metrics miss for 4 weeks.
