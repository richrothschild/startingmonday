VA Daily Checklist (Mon-Fri)

Daily (must-do)
1. Build/refresh 20-30 targeted leads in HubSpot list for next sales block.
2. Ensure all new calls from yesterday have Otter summary pasted into HubSpot notes.
3. Queue draft social posts for founder approval (at least 2 days ahead).
4. Run review-request list from activated users and send follow-up reminders.
5. Update tracker files:
   - weekly-scorecard-template.csv
   - risk-register.csv

Monday
- Prepare weekly lead packs from Sales Navigator and Apollo.
- Verify booking link placement in all active outreach scripts.

Tuesday
- Prepare coach outreach list and first-touch drafts.
- Compile objection snippets from recent calls.

Wednesday
- Monitor HARO/Qwoted opportunities and draft responses.
- Prepare one podcast pitch batch (3 targets).

Thursday
- Verify UTM consistency in all outbound links this week.
- Update proof asset tracker (reviews, case snippets, mentions).

Friday
- Assemble Friday scorecard pre-read for founder + consultant.
- Flag any kill-criteria threshold risks.
