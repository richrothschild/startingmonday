Email Nurture Sequence (Trial + Pre-Trial)

Email 1 - Immediate (Welcome + first action)
Subject: Start here: your first 15-minute win
Body:
- Confirm value promise in one sentence.
- Ask user to add 10 target companies now.
- CTA: Build first brief.

Email 2 - Day 2 (Activation nudge)
Subject: Your next move to get signal, not noise
Body:
- Explain one signal pattern and why timing matters.
- CTA: Generate one brief from top target.

Email 3 - Day 5 (Proof)
Subject: What users do before they get traction
Body:
- Share one short customer vignette.
- CTA: Complete optimize review + compare output.

Email 4 - Day 9 (Objection handling)
Subject: "I can do this manually" - true, but costly
Body:
- Reframe speed and consistency value.
- CTA: Book 20-minute optimization call.

Email 5 - Day 14 (Decision checkpoint)
Subject: Keep momentum: lock your workflow
Body:
- Ask if user reached activation milestones.
- Offer recommended plan path.
- CTA: Choose plan or book quick consult.

Email 6 - Day 25 (Save + convert)
Subject: Before trial ends: keep your pipeline live
Body:
- Summarize what they built during trial.
- Present plan options plus alumni fallback if not ready.
- CTA: Activate paid plan.
