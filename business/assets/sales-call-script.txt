20-Minute Sales Call Script (Founder-Led)

0:00-2:00 - Context
- Confirm role, current situation, and urgency.
- "What changed in the last 60 days that made this important now?"

2:00-7:00 - Problem diagnosis
- "Where does your search currently break down?"
- "How many target companies are you actively tracking today?"
- "How are you preparing for first calls today?"

7:00-13:00 - Live value proof
- Build a mini target list together (3 companies).
- Generate one brief live for their top role.
- Highlight: timing signal, role framing, first action.

13:00-16:00 - Offer
- Recommend plan based on intensity.
- Present founding option if eligible.
- Set concrete 14-day activation outcome:
  - 10+ companies added
  - first brief complete
  - first outreach sequence started

16:00-19:00 - Objections
Common objections and responses
- "I can do this manually": "You can. The issue is speed and consistency every week."
- "Price": "One coaching hour is typically more than a month of Active."
- "Not ready": "Use trial to build infrastructure before urgency spikes."

19:00-20:00 - Close and next step
- Start trial now.
- Send payment link if ready.
- Schedule 7-day checkpoint before ending call.
