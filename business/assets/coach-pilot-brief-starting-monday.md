# Starting Monday Coach Pilot Brief

## Audience
Executive transition coaches and leadership-prep advisors who support senior operators during job transitions.

## Core Problem
Clients lose momentum between sessions, over-index on public applications, and struggle to convert networking and interview conversations into offers.

## Starting Monday Value
Starting Monday improves timing, targeting, and execution discipline by helping coach-client pairs:
- Identify likely role openings before they are broadly posted.
- Prioritize decision-maker outreach with context and contact paths.
- Track weekly actions so sessions focus on high-value coaching, not recap.

## Pilot Structure
Duration: 30 days
Scope: 2 active clients
Model: Coach-led implementation with weekly checkpoint

## Pilot Workflow
Week 1:
- Define target role(s), target company criteria, and outreach narrative.
- Stand up client workflows and weekly action cadence.

Week 2:
- Generate and prioritize hidden-opportunity target list.
- Execute first outreach sprint to decision-makers and warm connectors.

Week 3:
- Review responses, refine messaging, and run interview-prep alignment.
- Tighten value proposition and leadership narrative based on market feedback.

Week 4:
- Measure outcomes, document conversion points, and decide scale plan.

## Success Metrics
Primary:
- Qualified networking conversations booked.
- Hiring-manager conversations initiated.
- Interview conversion rate from targeted outreach.

Secondary:
- Coach prep/admin time saved per week.
- Client action completion rate between sessions.
- Client confidence score (self-reported baseline vs week 4).

## Commercial Model
Pilot fee: negotiated by seat and usage pattern.
Trial path: free trial available for pilot onboarding.
Scale path: convert to paid seat(s) after pilot KPI review.

## Decision Gate At Day 30
Continue if:
- Clear improvement in qualified conversation volume and conversion quality.
- Coach reports meaningful leverage in client execution and session focus.

Pause if:
- No measurable movement in conversation quality or interview progression.
- Client implementation discipline is consistently low despite support.

## Implementation Notes
- Coaches should position this as workflow leverage, not replacement coaching.
- Best fit clients are willing to do uncomfortable relationship-based outreach and iteration.
- The highest ROI comes when outreach messaging and interview prep are integrated into one operating rhythm.
