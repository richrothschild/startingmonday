# Starting Monday Recruiter Pilot Brief

## Audience
Independent executive recruiters and boutique retained-search firms with heavy business-development responsibilities.

## Core Problem
Recruiters often find opportunities too late, compete in crowded funnels, and spend excessive effort on low-probability outreach.

## Starting Monday Value
Starting Monday helps recruiters and search principals:
- Detect likely executive openings earlier through signal-based pattern recognition.
- Prioritize target accounts and relevant decision-makers.
- Improve BD timing so firms are in the conversation before role visibility peaks.

## Pilot Structure
Duration: 30 days
Scope: 1 to 2 recruiter seats
Model: recruiter-led with weekly review

## Pilot Workflow
Week 1:
- Define search vertical(s), role archetypes, and account-priority rules.
- Configure outreach strategy by role and buying trigger.

Week 2:
- Launch signal-prioritized account list.
- Execute first contact sprint to target decision-makers.

Week 3:
- Review replies, meeting rates, and role-emergence quality.
- Adjust sequence copy, account filters, and follow-up timing.

Week 4:
- Quantify opportunity pipeline impact and fit for scale.
- Finalize seat plan and partner/referral terms if applicable.

## Success Metrics
Primary:
- Decision-maker conversations initiated from pilot accounts.
- Qualified search opportunities created.
- Meeting-booked rate from prioritized outreach.

Secondary:
- Time-to-first-qualified-opportunity.
- Outreach efficiency (touches per qualified conversation).
- Share of opportunities identified pre-public posting.

## Commercial Model
Standard baseline: executive pricing starts at $199/mo.
Recruiter seats: partner pricing can be customized based on usage and rollout commitment.
Trial path: free trial available with KPI review checkpoint.

## Decision Gate At Day 30
Continue if:
- Pilot account targeting creates higher-quality conversations than existing methods.
- Recruiter sees measurable lift in early-positioned opportunities.

Pause if:
- No clear BD conversion advantage versus existing stack.
- Team cannot operationalize signal-driven targeting into weekly execution.

## Implementation Notes
- Best fit teams already have outbound discipline but need better timing and account prioritization.
- Starting Monday should complement, not replace, CRM and sourcing workflow.
- The highest leverage use case is getting into shortlists early with relevant, role-timed context.
